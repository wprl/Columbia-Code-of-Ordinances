Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-396 Violations.


    (a)     Every person convicted of a violation of any of the provisions of this division shall be punished as provided in section 1-8 of this Code, except that a fine shall not be less than five dollars ($5.00) if paid within fifteen (15) days of the violation and not less than fifteen  dollars ($ 15.00 ) if paid more than fifteen (15) days after violation.

    (b) Each hour that a violation of this division continues is a separate offense.

(Ord. No. 14882 § 1, 6-17-96; Ord. No. 19250, § 1, 10-2-06)
