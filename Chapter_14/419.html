Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-419 Parking limited as indicated by meter signal.


    No vehicle shall be parked in a parking space alongside of, next to, in front of, or behind which a parking meter is installed and maintained for a longer period of time than the time limit indicated by the signal on such meter, upon insertion of a coin therein.

(Code 1964, § 12.735)
