Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-328.1 Same -- Between 7:00 a.m. and 4:30 p.m.; Sundays excepted.

    When signs are erected, placed or installed within each block giving notice thereof, no person shall park a vehicle within or upon any of the streets or places hereinafter set forth between the hours of 7:00 a.m. and 4:30 p.m., except on Sunday:

    Eighth Street, east side, from Wilkes Boulevard to North Boulevard.

(Ord. No. 16536, § 1, 8-21-00; Ord No. 20699, § 1, 8-2-10)
