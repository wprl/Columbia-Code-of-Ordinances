Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-526 Manner of operation, generally.


    It shall be unlawful for any person to ride, operate or manage any motorcycle, motor scooter or motor bike other than in a seated position thereon with one leg on either side thereof, except that the provisions of this article shall not apply to vehicles with three (3) or more wheels.

(Code 1964, § 12.1600)
