Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-254 Corner cutting to avoid traffic control devices.

    No person shall drive across any private or public driveway, parking lot, alley or yard in order to avoid stopping at any intersection controlled by an official traffic control device.

(Ord. No. 19560, § 1, 6-18-07)
