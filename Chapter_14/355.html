Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-355 No pick-up or drop-off zones.

When signs are placed, erected or installed giving notice thereof, the following zones shall be designated as no pick-up or drop-off zones and no person driving a motor vehicle shall drop-off or pick-up passengers, between 10:00 p.m. and 2:30 a.m., in any place marked as a no pick-up or drop-off zone:

Broadway, both sides, between Seventh Street and Short Street.

Cherry Street, both sides, between Ninth Street and Tenth Street.

Hitt Street, both sides, between Broadway and Cherry.  

Ninth Street, both sides, between Walnut Street and University Avenue.  

Sixth Street, both sides, between Broadway and Walnut Street.

Tenth Street, both sides, between Broadway and Locust Street.

(Ord. No. 21231, § 1, 2-6-12)
