Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-295 Taxi stands designated.


     When signs are placed, erected or installed giving notice thereof, the following zones shall be designated as stands for designated vehicles only and no person shall park a vehicle other than a taxi , limousine, bus or STRIPES vehicle between 9:00 p.m. and 2:30 a.m. :

Broadway, north side, between Waugh Street and a point 280 feet east of Waugh Street.

Cherry Street, south side, between Hitt Street and Tenth Street.

Locust Street, north side, between Ninth Street and Tenth Street.

Ninth Street, west side, first four spaces south of Elm Street.

    Tenth Street, west side, between Broadway and the alleyway just north of Broadway  .



(Code 1964, § 12.566; Ord. No. 14883 § 1, 6-17-96; Ord. No. 20388, § 1, 8-17-09; Ord. No. 21231, § 1, 2-6-12)
