Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-335 Reserved parking for police vehicles.


    (a) When signs are placed, erected or installed giving notice thereof, it shall be unlawful for any person to park a vehicle other than a Columbia Police Department vehicle at any time on the east side of Sixth Street, from the south curbline of Walnut Street to a point sixty-five (65) feet south thereof.   The police department shall pay the parking utility a fee of five hundred twenty dollars ($520.00) per year (or monthly proration thereof) for this space.

     (b)     When signs are placed, erected or installed giving notice thereof, it shall be unlawful for any person to park a vehicle other than a Columbia police department vehicle at any time on the twenty-five (25) foot section of curb on the east side of Ninth Street between Broadway and Walnut immediately south of the east-west alley.   The police department shall pay the parking utility a fee of five hundred twenty dollars ($520.00) per year (or monthly proration thereof) for this space.

    (c)    When signs are placed, erected or installed giving notice thereof, it shall be unlawful for any person to park a vehicle other than a Columbia Police Department vehicle at any time on the south side of Paquin Street, from the west curbline of Matthews Street to a point eighty-five (85) feet west thereof.

    (d)    When signs are placed, erected or installed giving notice thereof, it shall be unlawful for any person to park a vehicle other than a Columbia police department vehicle at any time on the west side of Tenth Street from the curbline of Alley A to a point twenty-three (23) feet north thereof.

    (e)    When signs are placed, erected or installed giving notice thereof, it shall be unlawful for any person to park a vehicle other than a Columbia police department vehicle at any time on the east side of Tenth Street, between the Bank of America parking lot to a point twenty-three (23) feet north thereof.

    (f)    When signs are placed, erected or installed giving notice thereof, it shall be unlawful for any person to park a vehicle other than a Columbia police department vehicle at any time on the west side of Ninth Street, from the south curbline of Elm Street to a point thirty-eight (38) feet south thereof.

    ( g )    When signs are placed, erected or installed giving notice thereof, it shall be unlawful for any person to park a vehicle other than a Columbia Police Department vehicle at any time on the north side of Parkade Boulevard from a point one hundred sixty-five (165) feet east of Bear Creek Drive to a point one hundred ninety (190) feet east of Bear Creek Drive.
