Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-475 Authority to establish zones of quiet.


    (a) The city traffic engineer is hereby authorized to establish zones of quiet near hospitals, as he may deem necessary.

    (b) Such zones of quiet shall be designated by placing signs or markings at conspicuous places in the street.

    (c) The use of horns and other sound devices on all vehicles is prohibited in such zones of quiet.

(Code 1964, § 12.185)
