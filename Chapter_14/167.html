Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-167 Crossing fire hose.


    No vehicle shall be driven over any unprotected hose of a fire department when laid down on any street, private driveway or streetc.ar track, to be used at any fire or alarm of fire, without consent of the fire department official in command.

(Code 1964, § 12.325)

     Cross reference(s)--Fire department generally, § 9-36 et seq.

     State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. § 300.305.
