Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-491 Definition.


    As used in this article, the word "cycle" shall mean any device upon which a person may ride; propelled by human power, having two (2) or more wheels,  excluding wheelchairs.

(Code 1964, § 12.1400; Ord. No. 14598, § 1, 8-21-95; Ord. No. 17642, § 1, 4-7-03)
