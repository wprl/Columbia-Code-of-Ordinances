Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-422 Depositing of slugs, damaging, etc., prohibited.


    No person shall deposit or cause to be deposited in any parking meter any slug, device or substitute for a coin of the United States, and no person shall willfully mar, deface, tamper with, destroy or impair the usefulness of any parking meter installed and maintained within the city.

(Code 1964, § 12.750)
