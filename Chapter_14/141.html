Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-141 Material falling from vehicles and trailers.

No person shall operate on any street or highway any motor vehicle or trailer that has rock or any other material on any part of the vehicle or trailer that may reasonably be expected to fall from the vehicle or trailer as a result of wind pressure or air pressure or by the movement of the vehicle or trailer.

(Ord. No. 17949, § 1, 1-5-04)
