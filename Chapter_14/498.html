Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-498 Same--Attachment of license or stamp.


    (a) Upon issuing a cycle license, a designee of the city manager shall attach to the frame of the cycle a license bearing the license number assigned to the cycle and the name of the city, or shall affix a permanent stamp to the cycle frame just above the fork on the right side or under the bottom bracket.

    (b) No person shall remove a license from a cycle unless the cycle is dismantled and no longer operated upon any street in the city.

(Code 1964, § 12.1440)
