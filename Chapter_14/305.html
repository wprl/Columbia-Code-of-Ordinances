Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-305 Emergency stopping only.


    When signs are placed, erected or installed giving notice thereof, no person shall stop, stand or park a vehicle in any such designated place as hereinafter provided, except in the event of an emergency:

     State Route 163 (Providence Road), from State Road 740 (Stadium Boulevard) to three thousand two hundred (3,200) feet south of Route 740.

     Vandiver Drive, both sides, from Providence Road to U.S. Highway 63.

(Code 1964, § 12.591)
