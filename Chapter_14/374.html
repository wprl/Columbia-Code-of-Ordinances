Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-374 Thirty-degree angle parking.


    All vehicles shall be parked at an angle of approximately thirty (30) degrees to the curb and headed into the curb on the following named streets:

     Broadway, north side, between Tenth Street and Hitt Street.

     Broadway, south side, between Fifth Street and Sixth Street.

(Code 1964, § 12.472)
