Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-504 Emerging from alley or driveway.


    The operator of a cycle emerging from an alley, driveway or building shall, upon approaching a sidewalk or the sidewalk area across any alleyway, yield the right-of-way to all pedestrians approaching on such sidewalk or sidewalk area, and upon entering the roadway shall yield the right-of-way to all vehicles approaching on such roadway.

(Code 1964, § 12.1510)
