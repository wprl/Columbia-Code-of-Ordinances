Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-336 No parking except school buses.


    When signs are erected, placed or installed giving notice thereof, no person shall park a vehicle other than a school bus within or upon any of the streets or places hereinafter set forth:

     Hickman Avenue, south side, Seventh Street to Eighth Street, between 7:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m., Saturdays, Sundays and other days school is not in session excepted.

     Hickman Avenue, south side, Fifth Street to Seventh Street, between 7:30 a.m. and 8:30 a.m., and between 2:30 p.m. and 3:30 p.m., Saturdays, Sundays and other days school is not in session excepted.

(Ord. No. 14390 § 1, 3-6-95)
