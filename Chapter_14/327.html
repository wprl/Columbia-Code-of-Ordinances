Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-327 Same--At all times; Sundays excepted.


    When signs are placed, erected or installed in each block giving notice thereof, no person shall park a vehicle at any time, except as may be herein provided on Sunday, upon any of the streets or places hereinafter set forth:

     Short Street, both sides, except that parking shall be permitted on the east side thereof on Sunday.

(Code 1964, § 12.600)

     State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. § 300.535.
