Section 14-223 Speed restrictions on city streets.

    (a)     No person shall drive a vehicle on any street at a greater speed than is reasonable and approved under conditions then and there existing.

All streets in signed school speed limit zones, with flashing yellow lights, when the yellow lights are flashing, not herein more particularly specified.

All streets in the University of Missouri Campus, as defined in section 14-1.
