Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-548 Impounding of abandoned or illegally parked vehicles--Authorized.


     Any police officer or parking enforcement officer may have removed to a suitable place any vehicle parked or left standing on a street, highway or public property for longer  than twenty-four (24) hours, or any vehicle parked contrary to the provisions of this chapter.  Unclaimed vehicles shall be disposed of in accordance with state law .

(Code 1964, § 12.670; Ord. No. 16077, § 1, 7-6-99)
