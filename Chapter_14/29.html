Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-29 Driver files to be maintained.


    The police department or traffic division thereof, shall maintain a suitable record of all traffic accidents, warnings, arrests, convictions and complaints reported for each driver, which shall be filed alphabetically under the name of the driver concerned.

(Code 1964, § 12.045)

     State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. § 300.045.
