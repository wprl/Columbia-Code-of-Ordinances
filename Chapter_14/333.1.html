Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-333.1 Same--Between 6:00 p.m. and 6:00 a.m.


    When signs are erected, placed or installed within each block giving notice thereof, no person shall park a vehicle within or upon any of the streets or places hereinafter set forth between the hours of 6:00 p.m. and 6:00 a.m.:

     Park Avenue, north side, from Trinity Place to a point one hundred thirty (130) feet west of Trinity Place.

(Ord. No. 12357, § 1, 9-18-89)
