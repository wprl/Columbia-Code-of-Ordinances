Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-78 Parking violations.


    In addition to the authority granted the traffic violations bureau by statute and court rule, the violations clerk or deputy clerk is hereby authorized to accept appearance, waiver of trial, plea of guilty and payment of fine and costs arising out of violations of the parking regulations established by this chapter.

(Code 1964, § 12.1360; Ord. No. 15125, § 1, 1-21-97)
