Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-328 Same--Between 7:30 am. and 6:00 p.m.; Sundays excepted.

     When signs are erected, placed or installed within each block giving notice thereof, no person shall park a vehicle within or upon any of the streets or places hereinafter set forth between the hours of 7:30 a.m. and 6:00 p.m., except on Sunday:

    Beverly Street, both sides, from Interstate 70 service road, north to Bernadette Drive.

    Berrywood Drive, south side, from Keene Street to Portland Street.

    University Avenue, north side, from College Avenue to Ann Street.

(Code 1964, § 12.605(B); Ord. No. 15025, § 1, 10-21-96; Ord. No. 16536, § 1, 8-21-00)
