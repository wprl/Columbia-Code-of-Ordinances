Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-304 No parking, tow-away zones.

    (a) When signs are placed, erected or installed giving notice thereof, no person shall stop, stand or park a vehicle upon any of the streets or places hereinafter set forth:

I-70 Drive Southeast, both sides, beginning two thousand (2,000) feet east of Woodridge Drive, thence east six hundred (600) feet.

Rollins Street, both sides, beginning 125 feet from the west curb of Hitt Street and ending 485 feet from the east curb of Missouri Avenue.

Any roadway signed as a snow route when there is two (2) inches or more of snow on the roadway.

    (b) Vehicles in violation of this section shall be towed.

(Code 1964, § 12.592; Ord. No. 16048, § 1, 6-21-99; Ord. No. 21841, § 1, 10-7-13)
