Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-478 Possession of official traffic control devices or railroad signs or signals.

    No person shall possess any City of Columbia official traffic control device or any City of Columbia railroad sign or signal without lawful authority and the written consent of the official having jurisdiction for placing or erecting such signs.

(Ord. No. 18961, § 1, 3-20-06)
