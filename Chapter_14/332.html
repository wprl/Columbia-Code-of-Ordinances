Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-332 Same--Between 7:00 a.m. and 4:30 p.m.; Saturdays and Sundays excepted.


    When signs are erected, placed or installed within each block giving notice thereof, no person shall park a vehicle within or upon any of the streets or places hereinafter set forth between the hours of 7:00 a.m. and 4:30 p.m., Saturdays and Sundays excepted:

     Clinkscales Road, east side, between Worley and Broadway.

(Code 1964, § 12.617)
