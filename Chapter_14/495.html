Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-495 Same--Application; assignment.


    (a) Application for a cycle license shall be made to a designee of the city manager upon forms provided by the city.

    (b) Upon the sale or other transfer of a licensed cycle the licensee shall, upon proper application, have such license transferred to the new owner.

(Code 1964, § 12.1420)
