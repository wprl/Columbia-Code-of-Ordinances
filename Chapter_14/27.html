Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-27 Obedience to police and fire department officials.


    No person shall wilfully fail or refuse to comply with any lawful order or direction of a police officer or fire department official.

(Code 1964, § 12.075)

     Cross reference(s)--Fire department generally, § 9-36 et seq.; police departments, § 21-16 et seq.

     State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. § 300.080.
