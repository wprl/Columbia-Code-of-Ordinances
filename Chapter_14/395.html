Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-395 Reserved parking; city vehicles.


    When signs are placed, erected or installed giving notice thereof, it shall be unlawful for any person to park a vehicle between the hours of 7:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m., Monday through Friday, in the following designated municipal parking lot spaces except clearly marked city vehicles:

    (1)    Municipal parking lot, the west three (3) spaces located south of the parking plaza and north of the alley north of Broadway between Seventh and Eighth Streets.

(Ord. No. 14461 § 1, 4-17-95; Ord. No. 14882 § 1, 6-17-96; Ord. No. 21195, § 1, 1-3-12)

    Editor's note--Section 3 of Ord. No. 12709, adopted Aug. 20, 1990, repealed former § 14-395, which pertained to a two-hour parking fee for certain unmetered off-street facilities and derived from Ord. No. 11857, § 1(14-394), adopted April 18, 1988. Ord. No. 14461 § 1, inserted a new section and was enacted 4-17-95.


(Ord. 21195, Amended, 01/03/2012, Prior Text)
