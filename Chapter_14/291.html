Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-291 Parking on right side of roadway.


    All vehicles not in motion shall be placed with their right-hand side as near the right hand side of the highway as practicable, except on such streets of the city where vehicles are obliged to move in one direction only or where parking of motor vehicles is regulated by ordinance.

(Code 1964, § 12.294(A))
