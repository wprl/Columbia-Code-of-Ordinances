Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-264 Obedience to no-turn signs.


    Whenever authorized signs are erected indicating that no right, left, U-turn or right turn on red is permitted, no driver of a vehicle shall disobey the directions of any such sign.

(Code 1964, § 12.310)

     State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. § 300.230.
