Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-411 Installation; hours of operation; parking time limited.


    Parking meters shall be installed and maintained on the streets and off-street parking lots set forth in this division, and no person shall, between the hours of 8:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m., on any day except Sundays and public holidays, park a vehicle in any parking space on such street alongside of, next to, in front of, or behind which a parking meter is installed, for a period of time longer than is indicated in this division for any street or off-street parking facility or lot established in this division.

(Code 1964, § 12.675)
