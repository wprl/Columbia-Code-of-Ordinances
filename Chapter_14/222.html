Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-222 State approval of city speed restrictions.


    The city will submit to the state highway commission, for approval, any ordinances, rules, regulations or resolutions appertaining to the regulation of speed where such ordinance, rules or regulations are applicable to state highways as determined in section 14-61(c), and will not enact or keep in force any ordinance not approved by the commission.

(Code 1964, § 12.206)
