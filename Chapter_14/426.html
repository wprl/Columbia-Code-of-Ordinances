Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-426 Violations.


    (a)      Every person convicted of a violation of any of the provisions of this division shall be punished as provided in section 1-8 of this Code, except that a fine shall not be less than ten dollars ($10.00) if paid within fifteen (15) days of the violation and not less than twenty-five dollars ($25.00) if paid more than fifteen (15) days after the violation.

    (b) Each hour that a violation of this division continues is a separate offense.

(Ord. No. 14882 § 1, 6-17-96; Ord. No. 19250, § 1, 10-2-06; Ord. No. 20869, § 1, 2-7-11)
