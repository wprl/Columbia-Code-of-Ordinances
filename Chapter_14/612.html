Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-612 Driving while intoxicated.


    A person commits the offense of "driving while intoxicated" if such person operates a motor vehicle while in an intoxicated or drugged condition.

(Ord. No. 13450, § 1, 9-21-92; Ord. No. 17051, § 1, 10-1-01)
