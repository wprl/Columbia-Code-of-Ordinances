Chapter 14 MOTOR VEHICLES AND TRAFFIC*


Section 14-613 Driving with excessive blood alcohol content.


    A person commits the offense of "driving with excessive blood alcohol content" if such person operates a motor vehicle with eight-hundredths of one percent or more by weight of alcohol in such persons blood.

(Ord. No. 13450, § 1, 9-21-92; Ord. No. 17051, § 1, 10-1-01)
