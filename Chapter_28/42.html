Chapter 28 VEHICLES FOR HIRE*


Section 28-42 Application.


    Application for a permit to operate a motor bus shall be made to the city council on a form to be prescribed by the city council. The city council shall fix a date upon which the council will have a public hearing to consider the application.

(Code 1964, § 11.168(2); Ord. No. 9919, § 1, 8-15-83)
