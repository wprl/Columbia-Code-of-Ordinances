Chapter 28 VEHICLES FOR HIRE*


Section 28-58 Amount of license tax.


    Every person engaging in, operating or conducting a public transfer business or delivery business under the terms of this article shall pay an annual license tax of fifteen dollars ($15.00) for one public transfer vehicle used in the business, and five dollars ($5.00) for each additional public transfer vehicle used in the business.

(Code 1964, § 11.250)
