Chapter 28 VEHICLES FOR HIRE*


Section 28-119 Administrative revocation.


    The business license administrator may, following the provisions of section 13-31 of the City Code, issue a provisional order or revoke a driver's certificate for any violation of this article or for other cause when he has reason to believe that the driver's actions are a threat to the safety or welfare of the citizens of the city.

(Ord. No. 12094, § 1, 12-12-88)
