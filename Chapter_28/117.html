Chapter 28 VEHICLES FOR HIRE*


Section 28-117 Duration and renewal.


    (a) Each driver's certificate shall expire on the thirtieth day of June.

    (b) Unless sooner revoked, renewals for driver's certificates for further years may be made on the basis of the original application. The director may from time to time require updated information in the event that the information shall appear to be out of date.

(Ord. No. 12094, § 1, 12-12-88; Ord. No. 17348, § 1, 6-17-02)
