Chapter 28 VEHICLES FOR HIRE*


Section 28-103 Driver uniforms.


    Each permit holder shall require every driver of a vehicle permitted to him while on duty to wear a shirt, jacket, vest, hat or other clothing article of uniform color and style with the permit holder's company name or logo clearly printed upon it which identifies the driver as a driver for the permit holder.

(Ord. No. 12094, § 1, 12-12-88)
