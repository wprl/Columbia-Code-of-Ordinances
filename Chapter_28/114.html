Chapter 28 VEHICLES FOR HIRE*


Section 28-114 Investigation of applicant.


    The department of finance shall investigate the qualifications and fitness of each applicant for a driver's certificate, including obtaining a criminal record check of each applicant.

(Ord. No. 12094, § 1, 12-12-88; Ord. No. 19518, § 3, 5-7-07 )
