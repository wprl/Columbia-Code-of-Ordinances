Chapter 26 TAXATION*


Section 26-31 Suit to be brought on forfeited bonds.


    Upon the forfeiture of any bond as herein provided, it shall be the duty of the business license administrator to report the same to the city counselor, who shall institute suit thereon without delay, against the principal and all the securities, jointly or severally, as may be deemed advisable.

(Code 1964, § 16.350)
