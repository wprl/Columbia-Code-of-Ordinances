Chapter 26 TAXATION*


Section 26-174 Administration.


    The stormwater development charge shall be paid to the community development department, division of   building and site development . The division shall promptly forward all stormwater development charge receipts to the finance department to be deposited in the stormwater utility fund.

(Ord. No. 13591 § 1, 2-15-93; Ord. No. 21095, § 11, 9-19-11)
