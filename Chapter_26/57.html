Chapter 26 TAXATION*


Section 26-57 Oaths and affidavits.


    The business services administrator, or employees or agents duly designated and authorized by the business services administrator shall have power to administer oaths and take affidavits in relation to any matter or proceedings in the exercise of their powers and duties under this article.

(Ord. No. 15047, § 1, 11-18-96)
