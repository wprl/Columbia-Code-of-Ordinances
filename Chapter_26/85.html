Chapter 26 TAXATION*


Section 26-85 Same--Rules of procedure; officers.


    The board shall require a quorum of seven (7)  members and the affirmative vote of four (4) of its members to make any recommendation. It shall elect , from its membership,  a chair and such other officers as   it  shall provide.

(Code 1964, § 11.1443; Ord. No. 17658, § 1, 4-21-03)
