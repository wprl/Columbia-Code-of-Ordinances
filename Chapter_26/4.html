Chapter 26 TAXATION*


Section 26-4 City representation on county board of equalization.


    For the purpose of giving the city representation on the county board of equalization when such board is sitting for the purpose of equalizing the assessment of property in the city, such officials of the city as may be provided by law shall sit with the county board of equalization, shall have a vote on such board, and shall be paid for such services, as may be provided for by law.

(Code 1964, § 16.050)

     State law reference(s)--Representation of certain constitutional charter cities, RSMo. § 138.015.
