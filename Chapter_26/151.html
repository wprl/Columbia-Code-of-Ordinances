Chapter 26 TAXATION*


Section 26-151 Imposition of charge.


    Every person issued a building permit for new construction shall pay a development charge of fifty cents ($0.50) per square foot of total floor area of new construction.

(Ord. No. 12175, § 1, 3-6-89; Ord. No. 17437, § 1, 9-16-02; Ord. No. 18789, § 1, 11-21-05; Ord. No. 19679, § 1, 9-17-07; Ord. No. 20416, § 1, 9-21-09)

Editor's note - Ord. No. 20416, § 1, adopted September 21, 2009, which amended section 26-151 above, shall be in full force and effect from and after January 1, 2010.
