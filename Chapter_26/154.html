Chapter 26 TAXATION*


Section 26-154 Administration.


    The development charge shall be paid to the community development department, division of building and site development . The division shall promptly forward all development charge receipts to the finance department to be deposited in a special fund and administered in accordance with the provisions of this Code.  

(Ord. No. 12175, § 1, 3-6-89; Ord. No. 21095, § 11, 9-19-11)
