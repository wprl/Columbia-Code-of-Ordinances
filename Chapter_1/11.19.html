Chapter 1 GENERAL PROVISIONS


Section 1-11.19 September, 2013 Extension of Corporate Limits.

The corporate limits of the City of Columbia shall include the following land:

A TRACT OF LAND LOCATED IN THE NORTHWEST QUARTER OF SECTION 1, TOWNSHIP 47 NORTH, RANGE 13 WEST, BOONE COUNTY, MISSOURI AND BEING ALL OF THE LAND SHOWN IN THE SURVEYS RECORDED IN BOOK 2920, PAGE 90, AND BOOK 3080, PAGE 187 AND DESCRIBED BY THE WARRANTY DEED RECORDED IN BOOK 4045, PAGE 119 AND CONTAINING 35.84 ACRES.

(Ord. No. 021788, § 4, 9-3-13)
