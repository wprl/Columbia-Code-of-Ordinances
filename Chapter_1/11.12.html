Chapter 1 GENERAL PROVISIONS


Section 1-11.12 February, 2013 Extension of Corporate Limits.

The corporate limits of the City of Columbia shall include the following land:

A TRACT LOCATED IN THE NORTHWEST QUARTER OF THE NORTHEAST QUARTER OF SECTION 34, TOWNSHIP 49 NORTH, RANGE 12 WEST, BOONE COUNTY, MISSOURI BEING LOT 1 OF BRINKMAN SUBDIVISION AS SHOWN IN PLAT BOOK 28, PAGE 79 OF THE BOONE COUNTY RECORDS AND CONTAINS 2.55 ACRES.

        (Ord. No. 21608, § 4, 2-18-13)
