Chapter 1 GENERAL PROVISIONS


Section 1-11.09 December, 2012 Extension of Corporate Limits

The corporate limits of the City of Columbia shall include the following land:

A TRACT OF LAND LOCATED IN THE SOUTHWEST QUARTER OF SECTION 3, TOWNSHIP 47 NORTH, RANGE 13 WEST, BOONE COUNTY, MISSOURI, BEING FURTHER DESCRIBED AS FOLLOWS:

ALL OF TRACT 1 OF THE SURVEY RECORDED IN BOOK 3393, PAGE 64, OF THE RECORDS OF BOONE COUNTY, MISSOURI.

(Ord. No. 21520, § 4, 12-3-12)
