Chapter 1 GENERAL PROVISIONS


Section 1-11.02 November, 2011 Extension of Corporate Limits

The corporate limits of the City of Columbia shall include the following land:

A TRACT OF LAND LOCATED IN THE SOUTHEAST QUARTER OF SECTION 32, TOWNSHIP 48 NORTH, RANGE 48 WEST, COLUMBIA, BOONE COUNTY, MISSOURI AND BEING TRACTS 1, 2, 3, 4 AND 5 OF THE SURVEY RECORDED IN BOOK 881, PAGE 902 AND BEING A PORTION OF PARCEL ONE AS DESCRIBED IN THE QUIT-CLAIM DEED RECORDED IN BOOK 3744, PAGE 124 AND CONTAINING 157.59 ACRES.

(Ord. No. 21140, § 4, 11-21-11)
