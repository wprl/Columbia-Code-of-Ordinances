Chapter 1 GENERAL PROVISIONS


Section 1-3 Provisions considered as continuations of existing ordinances.


    The provisions appearing in this Code, so far as they are the same as those of the "Revised Ordinances of 1964" and ordinances existing at the time of the effective date of this Code, shall be considered as continuations thereof and not as new enactments.

(Code 1964, § 1.020)
