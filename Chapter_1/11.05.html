Chapter 1 GENERAL PROVISIONS


Section 1-11.05 April, 2012 Extension of Corporate Limits.

The corporate limits of the City of Columbia shall include the following land:

Tract One
A tract located in the northeast quarter of Section 33, Township 48 North, Range 13 West, Boone County, Missouri being more particularly described as follows:

Tract One (1) of Country Farms Subdivision Plat 3 as shown by the plat recorded in Plat Book 40, Page 71, Records of Boone County, Missouri.

Tract Two
A tract located in the northeast quarter of Section 33, Township 48 North, Range 13 West, Boone County, Missouri being more particularly described as follows:

Tract two (2) of Country Farms Subdivision Plat 3 as shown by the plat recorded in Plat Book 40, Page 71, Records of Boone County, Missouri.

(Ord. No. 21292, § 4, 4-16-12)
