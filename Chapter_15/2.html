Chapter 15 MUNICIPAL COURT*


Section 15-2 Jurisdiction; change of venue.


    The municipal division of the circuit court shall have original jurisdiction to hear and determine all violations against the charter and the ordinances of the city. There shall be no change of venue or other transfer of cases to other divisions except as provided by the laws of the state. This section shall in no way be construed to remove or limit a defendant's right to trial by jury.

(Code 1964, § 7.025)
