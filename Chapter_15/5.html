Chapter 15 MUNICIPAL COURT*


Section 15-5 Sessions.


    The municipal court shall be open every day except Saturday, Sunday and legal holidays; provided, however, that the municipal judge shall have the authority to cancel municipal court for a date certain by giving proper notice to the court personnel, the city prosecutor, and heads of departments of the city, when he determines that the judicial business on such date will be slight and that rescheduling cases will create no hardship on any party.

(Code 1964, § 7.045)
