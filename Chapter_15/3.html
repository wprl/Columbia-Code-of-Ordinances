Chapter 15 MUNICIPAL COURT*


Section 15-3 Rules governing procedure and practice.


    The rules governing the procedure and practice in the municipal court shall be those established and promulgated by the Supreme Court of Missouri on July 1, 1959, and such subsequent rules as the supreme court shall from time to time establish and promulgate; and any provision of this chapter which shall be in conflict with such rules is hereby repealed.

(Code 1964, § 7.075)
