Chapter 15 MUNICIPAL COURT*


Section 15-27 Place of imprisonment.

    In all cases where any person is in the charge of or in the custody of the chief of police or members of the police department, either before or after trial, such person shall be kept and held at the Boone County jail or at such other place as the chief of police may designate, except as otherwise provided by law or ordinance.

(Code 1964, § 7.170)
