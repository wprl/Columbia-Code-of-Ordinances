Chapter 15 MUNICIPAL COURT*


Section 15-26 Disposition of fines and costs.


    All fines and costs paid into the municipal court shall be paid to and deposited not less frequently than monthly into the municipal treasury.

    All fines, penalties and forfeitures arising out of violations of ordinances of the city shall be deposited in the general fund of the city.

(Code 1964, § 7.165)

     State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. § 479.080(1).
