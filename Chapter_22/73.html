Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-73 When interested party meetings not required.


     Not withstanding any other provisions of this code, interested party meetings shall not be required for any of the following:

    (1)    Ordinary maintenance of public improvements.

    (2)    Projects where there are no obvious interested parties.

(Code 1964, § 14.380; Ord. No. 20880, § 2, 2-21-11)

    State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. § 88.520(3).
