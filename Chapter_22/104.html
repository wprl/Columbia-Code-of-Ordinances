Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-104 Special assessments limited to two dollars per front foot.


     (a)     The council may cause unimproved streets, avenues, alleys and other public places of the city, or any part thereof, to be sprinkled, oiled, repaired, surfaced and resurfaced, and the cost may be defrayed by a special assessment not to exceed two dollars ($2.00) per front foot.

     (b)    The council shall not levy special assessments under this section for any street maintenance project initiated after July 15, 2002.

(Ord. No. 10832, § 1, 12-16-85; Ord. No. 17377, § 1, 7-15-02)
