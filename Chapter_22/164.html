Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-164 City responsibility for items collected as refuse.


    The city shall not be responsible for damage, replacement, return or reimbursement of any items picked up as refuse which have been placed in refuse bags or placed at the normal location of refuse pickup.

(Code 1964, § 10.9120; Ord. No. 12820, § 1, 12-10-90)
