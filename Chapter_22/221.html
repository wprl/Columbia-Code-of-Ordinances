Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-221 Preliminary treatment facilities.


    Where preliminary treatment facilities are provided for any waters or wastes, they shall be owned and maintained continuously in satisfactory and effective operation by the owner at his expense.

(Code 1964, § 14.792; Ord. No. 10059, § 2, 2-6-84; Ord. No. 14914, § 1, 8-5-96)
