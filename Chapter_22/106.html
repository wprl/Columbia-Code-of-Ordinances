Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-106 Classification of streets; design standards and specifications.


    (a) For the purpose of planning the orderly extension, construction and reconstruction of streets, all public streets and rights-of-way are classified in the transportation plan as follows:

    (1)    Local residential;

    (2)    Local non-residential;

    (3)  Collector;

    (4)    Arterial;

    (5)    Expressway;

    (6)    Freeway.

    (b) Street design standards and specifications are set forth in the transportation plan and the subdivision regulations.

    (c) The council may, upon the recommendation of the director of public works, grant a variance or exception to the minimum street widths required by the standards and specifications.

(Ord. No. 10832, § 1, 12-16-85; Ord. No. 15506, § 1, 2-2-98)
