Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-215.1 Sanitary sewer discharge prohibitions.


    It shall be unlawful for any person to discharge or cause to be discharged any storm water, surface water, groundwater, roof runoff, subsurface drainage including interior and exterior foundation drains, cooling water, or unpolluted industrial process waters to any sanitary sewer.

(Code 1964, § 14.782; Ord. No. 10059, § 2, 2-6-84)
