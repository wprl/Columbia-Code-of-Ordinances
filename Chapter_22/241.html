Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-241 Definition; purpose; funding.


    Joint district sewers are those which have been or may be constructed or acquired under the authority of ordinances uniting one or more districts and unorganized territory, or uniting districts or unorganized territory into a joint sewer district, for the purpose of providing main, outlet or intercepting sewers, for the benefit of such joint sewer district, and paid for in whole or in part by special assessment upon the property in such joint , ;ewer district.

(Code 1964, § 14.690)
