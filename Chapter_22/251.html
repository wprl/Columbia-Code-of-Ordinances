Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-251 Definition; construction; cost to city.


    Private sewers are those paid for by private parties constructing the same. Private sewers connected with the public, district or joint district sewers may be constructed under such restrictions and regulations as the council may prescribe by general ordinance; but the city shall bear no expense for the construction, repairing or cleaning of same.

(Code 1964, § 14.760)
