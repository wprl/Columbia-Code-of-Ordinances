Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-227 Special tax.


    The council may levy a tax on all property made taxable for state purposes over the whole city, to pay for the constructing, reconstructing and repairing of such work, which tax shall be called "special public sewer tax," and shall be such amount as may be required for the sewer provided by ordinance to be built; and the fund arising from such tax shall be appropriated wholly to the constructing, reconstructing and repairing of such sewer.

(Code 1964, § 14.650)
