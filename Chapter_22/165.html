Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-165 Unauthorized hauling of garbage.


     (a)     It shall be unlawful for any person, other than an agent of the city, to haul, convey, carry or transport any garbage from any location within the city.

(b)    Notwithstanding the provisions of subsection (a), any generator of garbage that had been hauling its own garbage with the written permission of the director before April 7, 2003, shall be allowed to continue hauling its own garbage from its point of origin to a properly licensed disposal site.

(Code 1964, § 10.9130; Ord. No. 12820, § 1, 12-10-90; Ord. No. 17636, § 2, 4-7-03)

    Note--See the editor's note following § 22-158.
