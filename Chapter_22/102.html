Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-102 Liability of public property for tax bills.


    All lands owned by any state, county or other political subdivision, cemetery company or railroad company fronting or abutting on any public improvement shall be liable for their proportionate part of the cost of such improvement, and tax bills shall be issued against such property.

(Ord. No. 10832, § 1, 12-16-85)
