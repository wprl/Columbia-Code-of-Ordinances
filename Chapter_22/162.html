Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-162 Billing and credit.


    All service fees provided for by this article and those uniform utility charges set by chapter 27, article II, shall be billed and collected as provided for by the procedures of chapter 27, article II. All service fees collected shall be credited to the account of the department of public works.

(Code 1964, § 10.9100; Ord. No. 12820, § 1, 12-10-90)

     Note--See the editor's note following § 22-158.
