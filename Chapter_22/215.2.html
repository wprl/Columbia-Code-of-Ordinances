Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-215.2 Discharge into storm sewers.


    Storm water and all other unpolluted drainage shall be discharged to such sewers as are specifically designated as storm sewers, or to a natural outlet approved by the director. Acceptable industrial cooling water or unpolluted process waters may be discharged upon approval by the director to a storm sewer or to a natural outlet. An NPDES permit may be required.

(Code 1964, § 14.784; Ord. No. 10059, § 2, 2-6-84; Ord. No. 14914, § 1, 8-5-96)
