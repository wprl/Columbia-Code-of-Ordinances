Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-268 Notification.


    All users of the system shall be notified at least annually in conjunction with a regular billing for sewage service as to:

    (1)    The rate schedule in effect.

    (2)    That part of user charges attributable to wastewater treatment services.

(Code 1964, § 14.890; Ord. No. 14914, § 1, 8-5-96)
