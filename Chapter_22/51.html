Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-51 Condemnation proceedings.


    All proceedings for the condemnation or the damaging of property or in the exercise of the right of eminent domain shall be in accordance with the laws of the state now or hereafter applicable to cities of the third class.

(Code 1964, § 14.340)

     State law reference(s)--Condemnation procedure, RSMo. §§ 88.010--88.077.
