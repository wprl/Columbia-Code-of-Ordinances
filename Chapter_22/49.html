Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-49 Condemnation and removal of sidewalks.


    The city council may, by ordinance, condemn wooden or defective sidewalks, or any sidewalk which may be deemed unfit for use, and provide for the removal of any sidewalk so condemned.

(Code 1964, § 14.320)

     State law reference(s)--Condemnation of sidewalks, RSMo. § 88.507.
