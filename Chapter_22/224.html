Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-224 Special agreements.


    Special agreements, arrangements or contracts may be entered into between the city and any industrial concern whereby industrial waste of unusual strength or character may be accepted by the city for treatment subject to the rate and cost as established by the city council.

(Code 1964, § 14.796; Ord. No. 10059, § 2, 2-6-84; Ord. No. 14914, § 1, 8-5-96)
