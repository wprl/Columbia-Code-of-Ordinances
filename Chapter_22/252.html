Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-252 Acquisition by gift, condemnation, or purchase.


    The city may acquire any private sewer by gift, condemnation or purchase and provide for reimbursement by special assessment, in the manner hereinbefore provided, to be levied against the property in the district or joint district for which such private sewer is acquired; and an ordinance making a private sewer a part of a proposed district or joint district sewer, may provide that the contractor shall pay for such private sewer at the price fixed in such ordinance, and the cost thereof shall be included in the total cost of constructing such district or joint district sewer.

(Code 1964, § 14.770; Ord. No. 10059, § 2, 2-6-84)

     Charter reference(s)--Similar provisions, § 83.
