Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-226 Establishment.


    Public sewers shall be established along the principal courses of drainage, at such points, to such extent, of such dimensions and under such regulations as may be provided by ordinance, and these may be extensions or branches of sewers already constructed or entirely new throughout, as may be deemed expedient.


(Code 1964, § 14.650; Ord. No. 19841, § 1, 3-17-08)

    Cross reference(s)--Definitions and rules of construction generally, § 1-2.


(Ord. 19841, Amended, 03/17/2008, Prior Text)
