Chapter 22 PUBLIC WORKS AND IMPROVEMENTS*


Section 22-166 Delivery of refuse to authorized areas.


    All refuse transported through any street or alley of the city shall be delivered to a disposal facility approved by the state. When delivered to a city disposal area it shall be dumped in a manner and in a place directed by the director or his authorized representative.

(Code 1964, § 10.9140; Ord. No. 12820, § 1, 12-10-90)

     Editor's note--Section 1 of Ord. No. 12820, adopted Dec. 12, 1990, deleted former §§ 22-166 and 22-167, which pertained to depositing refuse or rubber in streams, lots, streets, using incinerators, and using bags or containers of another, and derived from Code 1964, §§ 10.9150, 10.9160.

    Section 1 of Ord. No. 12820 also renumbered § 22-165 as 22-166. See the editor's note following § 22-158.


(Ord. 17498, Amended, 01/02/2003, Prior Text)
