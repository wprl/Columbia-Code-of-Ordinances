Chapter 27 UTILITIES*


Section 27-125 Same--Fire protection.


    No monthly charge for water shall be made for fire protection facilities, sprinkler systems, hose closets, etc.; provided, that any water used by such system is used for the sole purpose of preventing or extinguishing fires.   Metered water use from fire hydrants shall be billed at the residential rate in effect for the jurisdiction and the season when used.

(Code 1964, § 15.640; Ord. No. 18218, § 1, 9-20-04)

Editor's note - Ord. No. 18218, § 1, adopted September 20, 2004, which amended section 27-125 above, shall be in full force and effect from and after October 1, 2004.


(Ord. 18218, Amended, 09/20/2004, Prior Text)
