Chapter 27 UTILITIES*


Section 27-162 Energy efficiency incentives authorized.


     The city water and light utility is hereby authorized to institute a program of energy efficiency incentives , subject to the availability of funds for such purposes, for qualified customers of the water and light utility under the terms and conditions hereinafter established.
