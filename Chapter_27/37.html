Chapter 27 UTILITIES*


Section 27-37 Director--Appointment and qualifications.


    There shall be a director of the water and light department, who shall be an engineer registered under the laws of the state, trained and experienced in the operation and management of public utilities, and qualified to perform the duties required of him by the charter and ordinances of the city. He shall be appointed by the city manager.

(Code 1964, § 15.010)
