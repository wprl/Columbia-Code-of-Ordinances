Chapter 27 UTILITIES*


Section 27-137 Installation.


    No person shall install equipment for an air conditioning system or a refrigeration system which requires a connection to, or which will use water from the water system of the water and light department of the city, unless such equipment includes water conservation equipment such as cooling towers, evaporative condensers or other water cooling equipment which will allow reuse of all water with only the normal evaporative losses.

(Code 1964, § 15.660)
