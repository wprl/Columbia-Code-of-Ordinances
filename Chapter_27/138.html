Chapter 27 UTILITIES*


Section 27-138 Fees; permits; enforcement.


    The payment of fees and issuing of permits for installation of an air conditioning system or a refrigeration system shall be as provided for in chapter 6, article II, Building Code; article III, Electrical Code, and article IV, Plumbing Code; of this Code of Ordinances. The enforcement of this division and the assessment of penalties, if any, shall be as set forth in such chapter 6, articles 11, III and IV.

(Code 1964, § 15.670)
