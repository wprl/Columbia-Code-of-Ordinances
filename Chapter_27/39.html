Chapter 27 UTILITIES*


Section 27-39 Water and light advisory board--Appointment and terms of members.


    There shall be a water and light advisory board which shall consist of   five  (5) members appointed by the council. Appointments shall be made for terms of four (4) years and until a successor is appointed and qualified. Appointments to fill vacancies shall be for unexpired terms only.

(Code 1964, § 15.030; Ord. No. 14466, § 1, 5-1-95; Ord. No. 17658, § 1, 4-21-03)
