Chapter 27 UTILITIES*


Section 27-204 Administration.


    The Columbia Terminal shall be administered as a separate division of the Columbia Water and Light Department. All engineering review, administrative services, liaison between the Columbia Terminal, shippers and connecting Class I railroads, and all marketing promotions for the Columbia Terminal shall be provided by the water and light department.

(Ord. No. 12209, § 1, 4-3-89)
