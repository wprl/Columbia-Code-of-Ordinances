Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-60 Penalties for violation.


    Any person found guilty of violating any of the provisions of this article shall in addition to any other penalty imposed, be ineligible for a period of thirty (30) calendar days to apply for a new license.

(Ord. No. 10810, § 1, 12-2-85; Ord. No. 11482, § 1, 5-18-87)
