Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-45A ARTICLE III. ARMED GUARDS AND SECURITY GUARDS*


     *Editor's note--Ord. No. 10810, § 1, adopted Dec. 2, 1985, in effect repealed Art. III, §§ 13-46--13-61, relative to guards and security patrol officers and enacted in lieu thereof new provisions relative to similar subject matter which has been included herein at the discretion of the editor as a new Art. III, §§ 13-46--13-60. Former Art. III, §§ 13-46--13-61 derived from Code 1964, §§ 11.830--11.910, 11.930--11.990, and 11.1010.

     Cross reference(s)--General penalty for Code violations, § 1-8; private detectives, § 13-156 et seq.; municipal court, Ch. 15; police, Ch. 21.
