Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-418 Appeals.

    Any person aggrieved by the action of the business license administrator in the denial of a license or of the revocation of a license, may appeal the decision under the procedures set out in Section 13-31 of this code.

(Ord. No. 17023, § 1, 9-17-01)
