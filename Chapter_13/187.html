Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-187 Supervision of waste hauling activities.


    It shall be the duty of the director of public works to supervise or cause to be supervised the removal of the contents of privies, the contents of septic tanks and  manure, and all persons licensed or engaging in the activities of waste hauler shall be subject to his direction to time, manner and place of removal.

(Ord. No. 14915, § 1, 8-5-96; Ord. No. 17636, § 1, 4-7-03)
