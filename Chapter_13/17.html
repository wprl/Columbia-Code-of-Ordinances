Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-17 Scope.


    It is not intended by this article to repeal, abrogate, annul or in any way interfere with existing provisions of other laws or ordinances. Where this article imposes a greater restriction upon persons, premises, or personal property than is imposed or required by such existing provisions of law, ordinance, contract or deed, the provisions of this article shall control.

(Code 1964, § 11.020)
