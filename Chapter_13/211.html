Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-211 Records to be kept by business license administrator.


    The city business license administrator shall keep a record of the number of the waste hauling vehicle stickers or plates issued to each licensee, together with the make, model and motor number of the vehicle for which each was issued.

(Ord. No. 14915, § 1, 8-5-96)
