Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-62 Limitation to handguns only.

    Armed guards licensed under this article are expressly prohibited from carrying, wearing or otherwise possessing a rifle, shotgun, or any other projectile-type weapon except a handgun while performing the duties of an armed guard.

    All handguns used by armed guards shall be limited to the following calibre of ammunition:

    (1)    .38

    (2)    .357

    (3)    .380

    (4)    .38 Super

    (5)    .9 MM

    (6)    .45 ACP

(Ord. No. 11482, § 1, 5-18-87)
