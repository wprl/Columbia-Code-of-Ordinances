Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-190 Same - Parking.


    (a) Adequate facilities for garaging or parking waste collection vehicles on private off-street lots located in areas zoned other than for residential use are required if vehicles are housed within the city.  Parking facilities must be approved by the director of public works.

    (b) It shall be unlawful for any person to park a waste hauling vehicle on a public street, or on private property used for residential purposes, except while picking up items for hauling as a licensed waste hauler.

(Ord. No. 14915, § 1, 8-5-96)
