Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-98 Application; endorsement by chief of police.


    Every application for license hereunder shall be made in writing to the business license administrator and shall state where the business is to be carried on. Before any license shall be issued, the business license administrator shall secure from the police chief an endorsement on the back of the application therefor.

(Code 1964, § 11.1520)
