Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-47 License required.


    No person shall operate as an armed guard or as a security guard in the city without first obtaining a license, from the business license administrator.

(Ord. No. 10810, 1, 12-2-85; Ord. No. 11482, § 1, 5-18-87)
