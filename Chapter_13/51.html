Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-51 Time for test sessions.


    The above described test shall be provided by and administered by the chief within thirty (30) calendar days of the application for license.

(Ord. No. 10810, § 1, 12-2-85; Ord. No. 11482, § 1, 5-18-87)
