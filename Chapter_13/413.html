Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-413 Applicant license denial or issuance.

(a)    If the applicant has been convicted of a felony within the past ten (10) years, the business license administrator may deny the license after considering the nature of the crime committed in relation to the license sought and the conduct of the applicant since the violation.

(b)    Additional reasonable conditions may be imposed upon the issuance of a license. Permission to operate a temporary business may be refused if  the applicant is unable to comply with the requirements of this division and all other applicable laws and regulations.

(Ord. No. 17023, § 1, 9-17-01)
