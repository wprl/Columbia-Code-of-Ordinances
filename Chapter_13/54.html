Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-54 Issuance of license.


    If a person shall meet or exceed the standards described in section 13-50 above, the business license administrator may not deny that individual a license based on the criteria set forth in said section.

(Ord. No. 10810, § 1, 12-2-85; Ord. No. 11482, § 1, 5-18-87)
