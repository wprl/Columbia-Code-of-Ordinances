Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-26 Term of license.


    Every person engaged in any of the businesses described in this article shall pay the license fees herein named, which shall be annual, and which shall be issued from the first day of July each year until the thirtieth day of June of the following year. The business license administrator shall cause a notice to be mailed to each licensee on or before June 1, informing him that his license shall expire on June 30 of that year, unless otherwise renewed.

(Code 1964, § 11.120)
