Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-131 License deemed supplemental to business license; no fee.


    Prior to issuance of the license provided for herein, the dealer shall first obtain the city business license provided for in article II of this chapter. There shall be no additional charge for the issuance of this license.

(Code 1964, § 11.1340)
