Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-234 Appeals.


    Any person aggrieved by the action of the business license administrator in the denial of a permit as provided in section 13-229 of this article, or of the revocation of a permit as provided in section 13-233 of this article, shall have the right of appeal the decision pursuant to the procedure for appealing a license denial set out in section 13-31.


(Code 1964, § 7.3070; Ord. No. 19518, § 2, 5-7-07 )
