Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-299 Revocation of license.

Licenses issued under this division may be revoked by the business license administrator for any of the following reasons:
  1. Fraud, misrepresentation or false statement contained in the application for license.
  2. Fraud, misrepresentation or false statement made in the course of carrying on the business.
  3. Any violation of this chapter.
  4. Conducting the temporary business in an unlawful manner or in a manner that constitutes a breach of the peace or a menace to the health, safety or general welfare of the public.


(Ord. No. 17023, § 1, 9-17-01)
