Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-53 Appeal procedure.


    Any person aggrieved by the denial of any license issued under this article shall have the right to appeal the decision pursuant to the procedure for appealing a license denial set out in section 13-31 .

(Ord. No. 10810, § 1, 12-2-85; Ord. No. 11482, § 1, 5-18-87; Ord. No. 19518, § 2, 5-7-07)
