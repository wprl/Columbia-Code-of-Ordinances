Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-52 Notice of rejection.


    The business license administrator shall act upon each application under this article and, if disapproved, he shall notify the applicant within seven (7) calendar days of his action stating the reasons for denial of the license and include a copy of test results.

(Ord. No. 10810, § 1, 12-2-85; Ord. No. 11482, § 1, 5-18-87)
