Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-99 Revocation.


    The business license administrator shall have the power and authority to revoke any license issued under this article for any violation by a licensed pawnbroker, of any of the provisions or conditions contained herein; provided such license may be revoked only after the licensee shall have been notified in writing at his place of business, of the violations complained of, and shall have been afforded a reasonable opportunity to have a hearing thereon before the business license administrator.

(Code 1964, § 11.1580)
