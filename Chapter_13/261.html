Chapter 13 LICENSES, PERMITS AND MISCELLANEOUS BUSINESS REGULATIONS*


Section 13-261 Definition.


    For the purposes of this Article, "convenience store" means a place of business that is primarily engaged in the retail sale of groceries, or both groceries and gasoline, and is open for business at any time between 8:00 p.m. and 5:00 a.m.  "Convenience store" does not include a business that is solely or primarily a restaurant or that has more than ten thousand (10,000) square feet of retail floor space.  

(Ord. No. 14122 §1, 7-5-94)
