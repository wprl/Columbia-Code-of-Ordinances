Chapter 24 STREETS, SIDEWALKS AND PUBLIC PLACES*


Section 24-10 Damaging bridges, sidewalks, culverts, etc.


    Any person who shall remove, tear up or otherwise destroy or damage any bridge, culvert, sidewalk, pavement, crosswalk or stepping stones in or upon any street, thoroughfare or alley of the city, without having lawful authority to do so, shall be deemed guilty of a misdemeanor.

(Code 1964, § 14.090)
