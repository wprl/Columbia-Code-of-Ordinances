Chapter 24 STREETS, SIDEWALKS AND PUBLIC PLACES*


Section 24-122 Interference with city employees.


    It shall be unlawful for any person to prevent, delay or interfere with any employee or agent of the city while such employee or agent is engaged in the planting, cultivating, pruning, spraying or removal of any tree or other woody vegetation as authorized in this ordinance.

(Ord. No. 15457, § 1, 12-1-97)
