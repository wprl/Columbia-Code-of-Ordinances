Chapter 24 STREETS, SIDEWALKS AND PUBLIC PLACES*


Section 24-101 When permits required.


    No procession or parade, except a funeral procession, the forces of the United States Army or Navy, the military forces of this state and the forces of the police and fire departments shall occupy, march or proceed along any street except in accordance with a permit issued by the chief of police and other regulations herein which may apply.

(Code 1964, § 12.345)

     Cross reference(s)--Licenses, permits and miscellaneous business regulations, Ch. 13.
