Chapter 25 SUBDIVISIONS*


Section 25-51 Expressways and frontage roads.


    Sidewalks shall be required on both sides of expressways and frontage roads unless the council determines that potential or existing pedestrian volumes do not necessitate sidewalks to safeguard the public health, safety and general welfare.

(Code 1964, § 19.545(3); Ord. No. 10099, § 1, 3-5-84; Ord. No. 15847, § 1, 1-4-99)
