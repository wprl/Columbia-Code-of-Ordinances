Chapter 25 SUBDIVISIONS*


Section 25-39 Natural features.


    Natural features, such as trees, brooks, hilltops and views, shall be preserved wherever practicable in designing any subdivision containing such features. Artificial and natural lakes and wooded areas are to be preserved and encouraged as much as possible.

(Code 1964, § 19.543(5); Ord. No. 10099, § 1, 3-5-84)
