Chapter 25 SUBDIVISIONS*


Section 25-36 Self-imposed restrictions.


    Any restrictions on any of the land contained in the subdivision greater than those required by this chapter or other city ordinances, which in the opinion of the director may affect the division and use of the land as contemplated by the subdivider, shall be indicated on the subdivision plat by a statement of such restrictions or by reference to the recordation of such restrictions in the office of the county recorder of deeds.   Any such restrictions may be eliminated only by ordinance or resubdivision.  Such restrictions shall not be eliminated unless the council determines that removal of such restrictions will not be detrimental to any land in the subdivision or to any neighboring property.

(Code 1964, § 19.543(2); Ord. No. 10099, § 1, 3-5-84; Ord. No. 19049, § 1, 6-5-06)
