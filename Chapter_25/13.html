Chapter 25 SUBDIVISIONS*


Section 25-13 Enforcement.


    In accordance with the other provisions of Chapters 20 and 29 of this Code, the provisions of this section shall be administered by the director. This in no way removes the obligatory duties of the director of public works or any other person required by the charter or ordinances to participate in subdivision control.

(Code 1964, § 19.508(1); Ord. No. 10099, § 1, 3-5-84)
