Chapter 25 SUBDIVISIONS*


Section 25-40 Subdivision name.


    The proposed name of the subdivision shall not duplicate or sound like the name of any other subdivision, any existing or platted street, or any established business or development in Boone County. Whenever final plats of a subdivision are approved in steps or phases, each submittal for final approval shall be numbered consecutively.

(Code 1964, § 19.543(6); Ord. No. 10099, § 1, 3-5-84; Ord. No. 14481, § 1, 5-15-95)
