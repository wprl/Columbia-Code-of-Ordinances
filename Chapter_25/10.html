Chapter 25 SUBDIVISIONS*


Section 25-10 Conditions.


    Regulation of the subdivision of land and the attachment of reasonable conditions thereto is an exercise of the valid police power delegated by the State of Missouri to the city. The subdivider has the duty of compliance with reasonable conditions laid down by the commission and the council for design, dedication, improvement and restrictive use of the land so as to conform to the physical and economical development of the city, and to promote the safety and general welfare of the future lot owners in the subdivision and of the community at large; the subdivision of land being a privilege conferred through these regulations.

(Code 1964, § 19.507(1); Ord. No. 10099, § 1, 3-5-84)
