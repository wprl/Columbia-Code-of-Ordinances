Chapter 25 SUBDIVISIONS*


Section 25-34 Same--Materials.


    The tract split information required with an application shall be a survey of the property proposed for division. The survey must be prepared under the supervision of a registered land surveyor and attested to by his seal and signature on the survey.

(Code 1964, § 19.541; Ord. No. 10099, § 1, 3-5-84)
