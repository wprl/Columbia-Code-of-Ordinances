Chapter 25 SUBDIVISIONS*


Section 25-54 Lot frontage.


    Except as otherwise provided and specifically authorized, as in the approval of a planned unit development or other special district, all lots, tracts or parcels hereafter created shall have actual frontage upon a street.

(Code 1964, § 19.560(2); Ord. No. 10099, § 1, 3-5-84)
