Chapter 12A LAND PRESERVATION


Section 12A-131 Compliance with permit terms.


    It shall be unlawful to dump clean fill or to allow the dumping of clean fill or to maintain a clean fill dump site in any manner which is in violation of the terms or conditions of the clean fill permit or in any manner other than in conformity with the methods set out in an approved clean fill permit application.

(Ord. No. 13374, § 1, 7-20-92)
