Chapter 12A LAND PRESERVATION


Section 12A-92 As built drawings.


    As built construction drawings that show the final design specifications and are certified by a professional engineer are required on all permanent stormwater management facilities.  The as built drawings must be submitted before city approval or acceptance of the facilities.

(Ord. No. 13019, § 1, 7-1-91; Ord. No. 19442, § 2, 3-5-07)
