Chapter 12A LAND PRESERVATION


Section 12A-84A ARTICLE V. STORM WATER MANAGEMENT

Editor's note - Ord. No. 19442, § 2, adopted March 5, 2007, repealed former Art. V, in its entirety, and enacted provisions designated as a new Art. V to read as herein set out.  Former Art. V was entitled, "Storm Water Management Requirements."
