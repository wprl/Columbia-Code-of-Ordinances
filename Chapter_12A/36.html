Chapter 12A LAND PRESERVATION


Section 12A-36 Application fee.

    An application for a land disturbance permit shall be accompanied by a non-refundable fee of two hundred dollars ($200.00).  The requirement of this section shall not apply to plot plan applications for sites one (1) acre or less or for individual lots in R-1 and R-2 zoned developments.

(Ord No. 18216, § 1, 9-20-04)

Editor's note - Ord. No. 18216, § 1, adopted September 20, 2004, which added section 12A-36 above, shall be in full force and effect from and after October 1, 2004.


(Ord. 18216, Amended, 09/20/2004, Prior Text)
