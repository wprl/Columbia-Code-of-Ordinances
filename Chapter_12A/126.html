Chapter 12A LAND PRESERVATION


Section 12A-126 Permits required.


    It shall be unlawful to dump, assist in the dumping, direct, solicit or allow the dumping of clean fill at any site within the city unless a clean fill permit for such dumping has been issued by the director. A permit shall not be required to dump topsoil for landscaping or gardening purposes.

(Ord. No. 13374, § 1, 7-20-92)
