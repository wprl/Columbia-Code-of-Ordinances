Chapter 12A LAND PRESERVATION


Section 12A-130 Hours of operation.


    It shall be unlawful to interfere with or disturb the peace and quiet of neighboring inhabitants by dumping in a clean fill site other than between the hours of 7:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m. on weekdays and between 9:00 a.m. and 5:00 p.m. on Saturdays. Nothing in this section shall prohibit dumping in a clean fill site in connection with disaster cleanup or in connection with work to maintain or restore utility service.

(Ord. No. 13374, § 1, 7-20-92)
