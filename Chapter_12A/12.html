Chapter 12A LAND PRESERVATION


Section 12A-12 Remedies not exclusive.

    The remedies set forth in this chapter are cumulative and not exclusive. The city may pursue any available civil remedies in addition to prosecuting violations in municipal court and following the abatement procedures of this chapter.

( Ord. No. 19442, § 1, 3-5-07)
