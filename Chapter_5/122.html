Chapter 5 ANIMALS AND FOWL*


Section 5-122 Notice.

    The department shall give notice to the person entitled to a hearing to contest an action taken or proposed to be taken by the department or the director.  The notice shall inform the owner of the right and manner of requesting a hearing to contest the action

(Ord. No. 21015, § 1, 7-5-11)
