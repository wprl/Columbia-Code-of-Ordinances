Chapter 5 ANIMALS AND FOWL*


Section 5-124 Request for hearing.

    The person entitled to a hearing may request a hearing before the director to contest the action of the department or director.  The request must be in writing and received by the director within five (5) days of the owner’s receipt of the notice of the right to a hearing of the animal. The request for hearing must state an address to which a notice of hearing may be sent.

(Ord. No. 21015, § 1, 7-5-11)
