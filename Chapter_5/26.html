Chapter 5 ANIMALS AND FOWL*


Section 5-26 City pound.


    The health officer shall, with the advice of the board of health, establish a city pound, under the supervision and direction of the animal control officer, for the reception and humane care of sick, injured, diseased or impounded animals, and establish rules and regulations therefor. The city may use the animal shelter operated by the Columbia Humane Society under terms of a contract or agreement to be approved by both parties.

(Code 1964, § 5.030; Ord. No. 11910, § 1, 6-6-88)
