Chapter 5 ANIMALS AND FOWL*


Section 5-15 Same--Director may delegate certain powers.


    (a) In order to remove or disperse congregations or flocks of birds constituting a nuisance as defined by ordinance, the director may delegate the implementation of a dispersal plan to any responsible citizen or citizens in the affected neighborhood by permit specifying the name of the permittee, date of issuance, date of expiration and details of the plan so delegated.

    (b)    This section shall not be construed to empower the director to authorize any private citizen to discharge any firearm or to use any device which launches a projectile of any type.

(Code 1964, § 5.057; Ord. No. 10427, § 1, 1-21-85; Ord. No. 21015, § 1, 7-5-11)
