Chapter 5 ANIMALS AND FOWL*


Section 5-56 Barking, annoying dogs.


    No person shall own, keep or harbor any dog which, by loud, continual or frequent barking, howling or yelping, shall annoy or disturb any neighborhood or any person, or which habitually barks at or chases pedestrians, or vehicles, whatsoever, to the annoyance of such pedestrian or drivers of such vehicles; provided, however, that this section shall not apply to the city dog pound, veterinary offices and hospitals, or licensed kennels or pet shops.

(Code 1964, § 5.120; Ord. No. 11910, § 1, 6-6-88)

     Cross reference(s)--Noise regulations generally, § 16-256 et seq.
