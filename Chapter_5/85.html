Chapter 5 ANIMALS AND FOWL*


Section 5-85 Loading, transporting, etc.


    Any person who shall, within the city, load, unload or transfer, from one vehicle to another vehicle, any hogs, sheep, cattle or other livestock in any public place, street or thoroughfare or on any private premises, and any person who shall park or stand any vehicle in which hogs, sheep, cattle or other livestock are loaded on any public place, street or thoroughfare or on any unenclosed private premises for a longer period than one hour shall, upon conviction, be deemed guilty of a misdemeanor and punished as provided in section 1-8 of this Code; provided, however, that nothing herein contained shall prohibit loading, unloading or transferring of hogs, sheep, cattle or other livestock at any established and maintained stockyard, slaughterhouse, stable, barns, or in any enclosed building.

(Code 1964, § 5.110)
