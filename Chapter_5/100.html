Chapter 5 ANIMALS AND FOWL*


Section 5-100 Nuisances.

    Any violation of this article that constitutes a health hazard or that interferes with the use or enjoyment of neighboring property is a nuisance and may be abated under the general nuisance abatement provisions of chapter 11.

(Ord. No. 20549, § 1, 2-1-10)
