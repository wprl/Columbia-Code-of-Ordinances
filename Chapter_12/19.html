Chapter 12 HUMAN RELATIONS*


Section 12-19 Human rights enhancement program.

    (a)    Upon the recommendation of the commission, the city manager is authorized to enter into agreements with local organizations for human rights enhancement activities.

    (b)    No program shall receive more than five hundred dollars ($500.00) in any fiscal year under this program and the program is limited to the availability of funds appropriated for the program.

(Ord. No. 20659, § 1, 6-21-10)
