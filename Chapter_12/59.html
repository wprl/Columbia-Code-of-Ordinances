Chapter 12 HUMAN RELATIONS*


Section 12-59 Penalty for violation of article.

    Any person who shall violate any provision of this Article shall be deemed guilty of a misdemeanor and shall, upon conviction, be punished by a fine of not more than one thousand   dollars ($ 1,000 ), or imprisonment not exceeding thirty (30) days, or by both such fine and imprisonment.

(Ord. No. 13194 § 4, 12-16-91; Ord.No. 17127, § 1, 1-7-02)
