Chapter 12 HUMAN RELATIONS*


Section 12-40 Acceptance of membership in real estate sales organizations.


    It shall be unlawful to deny any individual access to or membership or participation in any multiple-listing service, real estate broker's organization, or other service organization, or facility relating to the business of selling or renting dwellings, or participation, because of such individual's protected category or familial status.

(Code 1964, § 7.2060; Ord. No. 13657 § 1, 5-3-93)
