Chapter 3 AIRPORT*


Section 3-6 Rules and regulations for the operation and use of airport.


    The director, after consultation with the airport advisory board, may adopt rules and regulations for the operation and use of the airport. Such rules and regulations and amendments thereto shall become effective upon the approval of the council by ordinance and shall be posted in a conspicuous place or places at the airport. Violation of such rules and regulations shall be unlawful.

(Ord. No. 10665, § 1(3.060), 8-5-85)
