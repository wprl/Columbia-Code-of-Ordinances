Chapter 3 AIRPORT*


Section 3-5 Same--Responsibilities.


    The manager shall have immediate charge of activities at the airport. The duties of the manager shall include, but not be limited to:

    (1)    Managing the airport's business operations and financial functions;

    (2)    Promoting the development and efficient utilization of airport properties;

    (3)    Enforcement of all federal, state, and municipal laws and regulations at the airport.

    (4)    Supervision of all city employees at the airport.

(Ord. No. 10665, § 1(3.050), 8-5-85)
