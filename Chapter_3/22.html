Chapter 3 AIRPORT*


Section 3-22 Powers and duties.


    The board shall make a continuous study of airport needs and of aviation in the area, and shall make recommendations to the council for the development and use of the airport.  During June of each year, the board shall select a secretary, who shall keep a record of its activities and action, a chair, who shall be the presiding officer of the board, and a vice chair who shall preside in the absence of the chair.  The chair and vice chair must be members appointed by the council.

(Ord. No. 10665, § 1(3.100), 8-5-85; Ord. No. 16296, § 1, 12/20/99)
