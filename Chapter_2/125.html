Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-125 Financial interest in contracts.


    No officer or employee of the city shall have  any interest in any contract or  sale to the city of any land, materials, supplies or services  where said interest is in violation of the laws or constitution of the State of Missouri or ordinances of the City of Columbia . Any violation of this section shall render the contract or sale void, and any employee or officer so violating this section shall thereby forfeit his office or employment.


(Code 1964, § 2.620; Ord. No. 21701, § 1, 5-20-13)

    Charter reference(s)--Similar provisions, § 156.
