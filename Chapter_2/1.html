Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-1 Public clocks to use official time.


    All clocks or other timepieces in or upon public buildings or other premises maintained at the expense of the city shall be set and run according to the official time as provided in chapter 1 of this Code. It is hereby made the duty of the officer or other person having control and charge of such buildings and premises to see that such clocks or other timepieces are set and run in accordance with official time.

(Code 1964, § 2.023; Ord. of 4-4-66)
