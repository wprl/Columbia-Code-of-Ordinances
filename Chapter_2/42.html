Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-42 Quorum; calling meetings; adjournment; compelling attendance.


    A majority of the members elected to the council shall constitute a quorum to do business, but any two (2) members may have a call of the council or may adjourn from time to time, and may compel the attendance of absent members and make an order for their censure or fine.

(Code 1964, § 2.130)

     Charter reference(s)--Council to provide manner of compelling attendance, § 8.
