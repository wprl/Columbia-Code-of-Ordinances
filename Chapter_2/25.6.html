Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-25.6 Written policy on release of information.


    The provisions of sections 2-23 through 2-25.5 shall constitute the written policy of the city council and all City of Columbia public governmental bodies regarding the release of information on any meeting, record or vote. City public governmental bodies may supplement this policy with additional written policies not inconsistent with the ordinances of the City of Columbia.

(Ord. No. 11642, § 1, 9-21-87)
