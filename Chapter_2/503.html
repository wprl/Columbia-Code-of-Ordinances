Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-503 Expenditures.

a)    Columbia Trust funds shall be expended only after they have been appropriated by the city council.

b)    A restricted gift shall be used only for the purpose specified by the donor.  If any restricted gift of personal property or cash is used for a purpose other than that specified by the donor, the donor or the donor’s heirs or successors shall have the right to a return of the gift or its cash equivalent.

(Ord. No. 15993, § 1, 5-3-99)
