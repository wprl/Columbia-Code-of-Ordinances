Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-66 Punishment for violation.


    For violation of any of its rules or order of business, or for conduct at a meeting unbecoming a council member, the council may, by majority vote, punish the offender for contempt and fine him, for the use of the city, in a sum not exceeding twenty-five dollars ($25.00), to be recovered from him as any other penalty.

(Code 1964, § 2.370)
