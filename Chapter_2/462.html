Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-462 Procurement of architectural, engineering and land surveying services.


    The city council may, by ordinance, establish special procedures and guidelines for procurement of architectural, engineering and land surveying services.

(Code 1964, § 2.797, Ord. No. 10789, § 1(2.797), 11-18-85; Ord. No. 14459 § 1, 4-17-95; Ord. No. 15267, § 1, 6-16-97; Ord. No. 018178. § 2, 7-19-04)
