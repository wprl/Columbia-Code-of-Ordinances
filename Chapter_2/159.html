Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-159 Appointment of temporary clerk.


    Whenever for any reason the city clerk is absent or otherwise  unable to perform the duties of such  office, the deputy city clerk shall serve in such capacity and shall have all the powers and perform all the duties of the regular clerk.

(Code 1964, § 2.070; Ord. No. 21829, § 1, 9-16-13)
