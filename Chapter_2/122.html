Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-122 Board and commission members.


    (a) Residency required. All members of boards and commissions established by ordinances of the city, who are required by law or ordinance to be registered voters of the city, shall be residents of the city.

    (b) Removal. Any member of a board or commission of the city may be removed from such board or commission by vote of a majority of the members of the council.

(Code 1964, § 2.635; Ord. No. 10235, § 1, 7-16-84)
