Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-60 Previous question.


    The previous question shall be in this form: "Shall the main question be now put?" It shall only be admitted on demand of two (2) members and sustained by a vote of four-fifths of the members present, and until decided, shall preclude all amendments and further debate, and shall be decided without debate.

(Code 1964, § 2.310)
