Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-59 When motion to adjourn in order.


    A motion to adjourn shall always be in order, except: first, when a member is in possession of the floor; second, while the ayes and nays are being called; third, while the members are voting; fourth, when it was the last preceding motion; and fifth, when it has been decided that the previous question shall be taken.

(Code 1964, § 2.300)
