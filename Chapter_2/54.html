Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-54 Requirements for considering resolution.


    No resolution may be considered by the council unless it is in writing and has been prepared and approved as to form by the city counselor's office, had a resolution number assigned to it by the city manager's office, and has been placed on the agenda at or before the time for approval and adjustment of the agenda in the regular order of business as set forth in section 2-46.

(Code 1964, § 2.250)
