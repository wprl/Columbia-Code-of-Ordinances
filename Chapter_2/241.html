Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-241 Establishment; members; qualifications; appointment; terms.

    The bicycle/pedestrian commission is hereby established. The commission shall be composed of ten (10) members. One (1) member shall be a member of the parks and recreation commission chosen by that commission; one (1) member shall have knowledge of street construction and maintenance; four (4) members shall have knowledge of bicycling, bicycle safety, or bikeways; and four (4) members shall have an interest in pedestrian safety and infrastructure. All members shall be qualified voters of the city. The members succeeding the original members shall be appointed by the city council for three-year terms. The member from the parks and recreation commission shall be a voting member but shall not be eligible to serve as an officer of the bicycle/pedestrian commission.

Editor's note--Ord. No. 21571, § 1, adopted January 7, 2013, recodified Article V.  The previous applicable sections were Sections 2-250 - 2-253.
