Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-61 When motion to amend and debate precluded.


    A motion for the previous question, or to lay the question on the table, until decided, shall preclude all amendments and debate on the main question; and a motion to amend, or to adjourn it to a certain day shall, until it is decided, preclude all further amendment to the main question.

(Code 1964, § 2.320)
