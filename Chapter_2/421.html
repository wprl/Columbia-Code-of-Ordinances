Chapter 2 ADMINISTRATION*


Section 2-421 Office established; responsibility.


    There is hereby established an office of community services, which shall be a division of the health department . The office of community services shall be responsible for coordinating, monitoring, evaluating and planning for community human service programs for the city.

(Code 1964, § 2.1100; Ord. No. 18868, § 1, 1-17-06)
