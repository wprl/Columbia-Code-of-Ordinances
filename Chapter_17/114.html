Chapter 17 PARKS AND RECREATION*


Section 17-114 Alcoholic beverages prohibited in certain parks.


    (a)    Except as allowed in subsection (a), it shall be unlawful for any person to possess or consume any alcoholic beverage in any of the following city parks:

    (1)    Paquin Park;

    (2)    Flat Branch Park; and

    (3)    Village Square Park

    (b)    The definition of “alcoholic beverage” contained in chapter 4 shall apply to this section.

    (c)    This section shall not apply to any person possessing or consuming an alcoholic beverage at an event in a park where alcoholic beverages are allowed under a permit issued by the director under section 17-134.

(Code 1964, § 2.970(1); Ord. No. 20901, § 1, 3-7-11)

    Cross reference(s)--Alcoholic beverages, Ch. 4.
