Chapter 17 PARKS AND RECREATION*


Section 17-111 Appropriate clothing.


    It shall be unlawful for any person in a park to appear at any place in other than proper clothing. With the exception of the restricted bathing areas, "properly clothed" shall be construed to prohibit the wearing of trunks or clothing that does not cover the upper portion of the body.

(Code 1964, § 2.970(3))
