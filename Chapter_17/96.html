Chapter 17 PARKS AND RECREATION*


Section 17-96 Games.


    It shall be unlawful for any person in a park to take part in or abet the playing of any games involving thrown or otherwise propelled objects such as balls, stones, arrows, javelins or model airplanes, except in areas set apart for such forms of recreation. The playing of rough or comparatively dangerous games such as football, baseball and quoits is prohibited except in the fields and courts or areas provided therefor. Roller skating shall be confined to those areas specifically designated for such pastime.

(Code 1964, § 2.960(7))
