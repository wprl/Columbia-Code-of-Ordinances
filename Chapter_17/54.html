Chapter 17 PARKS AND RECREATION*


Section 17-54 Erection of structures.

    It shall be unlawful for any person in a park to construct or erect any building or structure of whatever kind, whether permanent or temporary in character, or run or string any public service utility into, upon, or across park lands, except or special written permit issued hereunder.

(Code 1964, § 2.930(1)(d))
