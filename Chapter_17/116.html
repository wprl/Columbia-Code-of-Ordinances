Chapter 17 PARKS AND RECREATION*


Section 17-116 Scott's Branch Trail; limitation on dogs.

    It shall be unlawful for any person owning or responsible for a dog to allow the dog to be on portions of the Scott’s Branch Trail between Dublin Park and the Bonnie View Nature Sanctuary.

(Ord. No. 21174, § 1, 12-5-11)
