Chapter 17 PARKS AND RECREATION*


Section 17-115 Bonnie View Nature Sanctuary; limitations on dogs and cycles.

    (a)    It shall be unlawful for any person owning or responsible for a dog to allow the dog to be in any portion of Bonnie View Nature Sanctuary south of Rollins Road and west of the Scott’s Branch Trail.

    (b)    It shall be unlawful for any person to ride a cycle, as defined in Sec. 17-140, in Bonnie View Nature Sanctuary other than on a hard surfaced area.

(Code 1964, § 2.970(6); Ord. No. 20901, § 1, 3-7-11 ; Ord. No. 20967, § 1, 5-16-11 )
