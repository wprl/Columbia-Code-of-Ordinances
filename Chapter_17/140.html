Chapter 17 PARKS AND RECREATION*


Section 17-140 Definitions and rules of construction.


    The following definitions and rules of construction apply to this division:

     City trail means any trail maintained by the parks and recreation department for use by pedestrians or cyclists.

     Cycle means any device, other than a wheelchair, which is propelled by human power and has one or more wheels at least twenty (20) inches in diameter and a frame size of at least fourteen (14) inches.

     Pedestrian includes any person riding in a wheelchair.

     Wheelchair means a chair mounted on wheels for use by disabled individuals.

(Ord. No. 14460 § 1, 4-17-95)
