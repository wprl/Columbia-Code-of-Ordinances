Chapter 8 ELECTIONS*


Section 8-33 Second Ward.

The Second Ward boundary shall begin at the intersection of the centerline of Oakland Gravel Road and Highway 63; thence southward along the centerline of Oakland Gravel Road to its intersection with Vandiver Drive; thence eastward along the centerline of Vandiver Drive to its intersection of Paris Road; thence southwestward along the centerline of Paris Road to the centerline of Interstate 70; thence westward along the centerline of Interstate 70 to where the centerline of Interstate 70 crosses over the centerline of Clinkscales Drive; thence southward to the intersection of the centerline of Clinkscales drive and I-70 Drive Southwest; thence westward along the centerline of I-70 Drive Southwest to its intersection with the centerline of Silvey Street; thence southward along the centerline of Silvey Street to the intersection with West Broadway; thence westward along the centerline of West Broadway to its intersection with Strawn Road; thence north along the centerline of Strawn Road to its intersection with West Broadway; thence westward along the centerline of West Broadway .4 of a mile;  thence northward .25 of a mile; thence westward to the city limits line, as now or hereafter established; thence in a clockwise direction along the city limits line, as now or hereafter established, to the point at which the city limits meet the centerline of Oakland Gravel Road, the point of beginning of this ward boundary.

(Ord. No. 13149, § 1, 11-4-91; Ord. No. 17075, § 2, 10-15-01; Ord. No. 21126, § 2, 1-17-11)

EDITOR'S NOTE:  Section 8-33 was repealed by Ordinance No. 21126 on 10-17-11.
