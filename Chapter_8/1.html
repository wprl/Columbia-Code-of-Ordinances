Chapter 8 ELECTIONS*


Section 8-1 Time of elections.


     Elections for council members shall be held annually on the first Tuesday after the first Monday in April in accordance with the City Charter.  The city council may call special elections in accordance with state law.

(Code 1964, § 8.020; Ord. No. 21092, § 1, 9-19-11)
