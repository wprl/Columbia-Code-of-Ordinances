Chapter 21 POLICE*


Section 21-31 Records of revenues.


    The chief of police shall compile such accounts of all money collected by or in his office as may be required by the director of finance, and shall report the same to the director of finance, and pay over all money collected by him as fees, fines, or from other sources, unless other disposition shall have been provided by Law or ordinances.

(Code 1964, § 7.260)
