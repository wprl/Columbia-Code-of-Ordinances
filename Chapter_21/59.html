Chapter 21 POLICE*


Section 21-59 Availability of police policies.

    The chief of police shall make available to the officers and the public, and place on the city’s website, all police department policies, guidelines, directives, orders, rules and regulations except those that would reveal tactics that would endanger the life of any police officer.

(Ord. No. 21157, § 1, 11-21-11)


(Ord. 21157, Added, 11/21/2011)
