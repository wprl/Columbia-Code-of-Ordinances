Chapter 19 PERSONNEL POLICIES, PROCEDURES, RULES AND REGULATIONS*


Section 19-222 Attitudes.


    Whenever work habits, attitude, production, or personal conduct of an employee falls below a desirable standard, supervisors shall point out the deficiencies at the time (or as soon as practicable) they are observed. Corrections and suggestions should be made in a constructive and helpful manner.

(Code 1964, § 22.1320)
