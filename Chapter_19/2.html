Chapter 19 PERSONNEL POLICIES, PROCEDURES, RULES AND REGULATIONS*


Section 19-2 Purpose.


    The purpose of these rules is the formulation of sound personnel policies designed to promote efficiency and economy, reward merit, provide for the impartial settlement of grievances, develop and maintain morale, and establish equitable non-discriminatory standards for the classification and compensation of city employees.

(Code 1964, § 22.020)
