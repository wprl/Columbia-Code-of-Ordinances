Chapter 19 PERSONNEL POLICIES, PROCEDURES, RULES AND REGULATIONS*


Section 19-109 Flexible benefits.


    All employees represented by the Columbia Police Officers Association shall be granted a benefit amount of thirty-one cents ($0.31) per hour, to be added to their regular rate of pay for obtaining equipment and life insurance. The above employees shall not be eligible for any other life insurance benefits established herein.

(Code 1964, § 22.662; Ord. No. 9936, § 1, 9-19-83; Ord. No. 12416, § 1, 11-20-89; Ord. No. 12746, § 1, 9-17-90; Ord. No. 15355, § 1, 9-15-97)
