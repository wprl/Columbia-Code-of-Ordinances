Chapter 19 PERSONNEL POLICIES, PROCEDURES, RULES AND REGULATIONS*


Section 19-38 Payments, gifts, etc., in connection with appointments and promotions.


    No person seeking appointment to or promotion with the city shall either directly or indirectly give, render or pay any money, service, or other valuable thing to any person for or on account of or in connection with his/her test, appointment, proposed appointment, promotion, or proposed promotion.

(Code 1964, § 22.210(3))
