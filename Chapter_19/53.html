Chapter 19 PERSONNEL POLICIES, PROCEDURES, RULES AND REGULATIONS*


Section 19-53 Classification reviews.

    (a)      All permanent city positions will be reviewed for appropriate classification on a schedule to be determined by the city manager.

    (b)      Reorganization, classification  reassignment , and new classification requests will be reviewed by the director on an annual basis, normally during preparation of the annual budget.     

    ( c )    Classification reassignment recommendations shall normally be considered by the city manager during preparation of the annual budget. The city manager shall not  reassign the classification of a permanent position unless the city council has provided for the reassignment in the authorized personnel section of the annual budget.

(Code 1964, § 22.430; Ord. No. 15958, § 2, 4-19-99; Ord. No. 17016, § 1, 9-17-01; Ord. No. 19230, § 1, 9-18-06; Ord. No. 21828, § 1, 9-16-13 )
