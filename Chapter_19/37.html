Chapter 19 PERSONNEL POLICIES, PROCEDURES, RULES AND REGULATIONS*


Section 19-37 Candidacy for public office.


     An employee of the city shall be placed on leave of absence under the provisions of this chapter after filing a declaration of candidacy for any public office. An employee, upon election to any public office, shall immediately resign the employee's  position with the city  as required by law .

(Code 1964, § 22.210(2); Ord. No. 21828, § 1, 9-16-13)
