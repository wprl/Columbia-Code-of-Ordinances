Chapter 19 PERSONNEL POLICIES, PROCEDURES, RULES AND REGULATIONS*


Section 19-52 Allocation of new classifications and positions.

The annual budget shall establish the permanent positions authorized for each department.  An employee shall not be hired, transferred or promoted to a permanent position unless the position is authorized in the budget.  The city council, by ordinance, may amend the budget to change the number and classification of authorized personnel.

(Code 1964, § 22.420; Ord. No. 15958, § 2, 4-19-99)
