Chapter 9 FIRE PREVENTION AND PROTECTION*


Section 9-4 Use of siren whistles limited.


    It shall be unlawful for any person to use, or for the owner thereof to permit to be used, any siren whistle which is not installed either on fire or police apparatus, or in some way pertaining to fire, without first obtaining permission from the fire chief.

(Code 1964, § 9.1610)

     Cross reference(s)--Motor vehicles generally, § 14-16 et seq.
