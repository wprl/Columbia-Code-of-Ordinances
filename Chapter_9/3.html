Chapter 9 FIRE PREVENTION AND PROTECTION*


Section 9-3 False fire alarms.


    Any person within the city who shall wilfully give, sound or make any false alarm of fire shall be deemed guilty of a misdemeanor.

(Code 1964, § 9.1600)

     Cross reference(s)--Offenses against public order, § 16-176 et seq.; offenses affecting government, § 16-221 et seq.; offenses against public safety, § 16-231 et seq.
