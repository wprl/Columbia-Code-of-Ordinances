Chapter 20 PLANNING*


Section 20-57 Urban renewal.


    All projects of the land clearance for redevelopment authority of Columbia shall be submitted to the planning and zoning commission for its study and recommendations and to the city council for its approval as provided by Chapter 99 of the Revised Statutes of Missouri, 1978, as amended or as may be amended hereafter.

(Code 1964, § 19.580)
