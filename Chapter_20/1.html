Chapter 20 PLANNING*


Section 20-1 Authority to establish building or setback lines on major streets.


    When a plan for major streets has been adopted by the city council, upon recommendation of the planning and zoning commission, the council may establish, regulate, limit and amend by ordinance, building or setback lines on major streets and prohibit any new building being located within building or setback lines.

(Code 1964, § 19.610)
