Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-66 Fines for misdemeanors and infractions.


    Except as otherwise provided for an offense outside this Code, a person who has been convicted of a misdemeanor or infraction may be sentenced to pay a fine which does not exceed:

    (1)    For a class A misdemeanor, one thousand dollars ($1,000.00);

    (2)    For a class B misdemeanor, five hundred dollars ($500.00);

    (3)    For a class C misdemeanor, three hundred dollars ($300.00);

    (4)    For an infraction, two hundred dollars ($200.00).

(Code 1964, § 7.520)

     State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. § 560.011.
