Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-303 Police order to disperse.

    Columbia police officers are authorized to order those attending a nuisance party to disperse. It shall be unlawful for any person not domiciled at the site of the nuisance party to fail or refuse to leave the premises immediately after being told to leave by a Columbia police officer.

(Ord. No. 19287, §1, 11-6-06)
