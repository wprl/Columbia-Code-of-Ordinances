Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-272 Blowers, power fans, etc.


    The operation of any noise-creating blower or power fan or any internal combustion engine, the operation of which causes noise due to the explosion of operating gases or fluids, unless the noise from such blower or fan is muffled and such engine is equipped with a muffler device sufficient to deaden such noise, is hereby declared unlawful.

(Code 1964, § 7.345)
