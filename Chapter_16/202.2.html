Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-202.2 Abuse of a police dog or police horse.


    (a)     A person commits the crime of abuse of a police dog  or police horse  if the person knowingly kills, assaults, beats, strikes, kicks, mutilates, injures, disables, arouses, angers or excites any police dog  or police horse.

    (b)     Abuse of a police dog  or police horse  is a class A misdemeanor.

(Ord. No. 14512, § 2, 6-5-95; Ord. No. 19072, § 1, 6-5-06)
