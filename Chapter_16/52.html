Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-52 Terms of probation.


    (a) Unless terminated as provided in section 16-56, the terms during which probation shall remain conditional and be subject to revocation are:

    (1)    A term not less than six (6) months and not to exceed two (2) years for a misdemeanor.

    (2)    A term not less than six (6) months and not to exceed one year for an infraction.

    (b) The court shall designate a specific term of probation at the time of sentencing or at the time of suspension of imposition of sentence.

(Code 1964, § 7.495)

     State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. § 558.016.
