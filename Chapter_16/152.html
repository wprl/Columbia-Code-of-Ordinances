Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-152 Negligent burning or exploding.


    (a) A person commits the crime of negligent burning or exploding when he with criminal negligence causes damage to property of another by fire or explosion.

    (b) Negligent burning or exploding is a class B misdemeanor.

(Code 1964, § 7.775)

     State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. § 569.065.
