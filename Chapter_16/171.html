Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-171 Stealing.


    A person commits the crime of stealing if he appropriates property or services of another with the purpose to deprive him thereof, either without his consent or by means of deceit or coercion. Stealing is a class A misdemeanor.

(Code 1964, § 7.825)

     State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. § 570.030.
