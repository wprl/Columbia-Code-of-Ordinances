Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-267 Hawkers and peddlers.


    The shouting and crying of peddlers, hawkers and vendors which disturbs the peace and quiet of the neighborhood is hereby declared unlawful.

(Code 1964, § 7.320)

     Cross reference(s)--Licenses, permits and miscellaneous business regulations, Ch. 13.
