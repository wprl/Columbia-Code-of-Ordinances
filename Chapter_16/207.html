Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-207 Escape from custody.


    (a) A person commits the crime of escape from custody if, while being held in custody after arrest for any crime, he escapes from custody.

    (b) Escape from custody is a class A misdemeanor.

(Code 1964, § 7.955)

     State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. § 575.200.
