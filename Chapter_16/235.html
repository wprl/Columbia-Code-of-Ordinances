Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-235 Barbed wire fences.


    No person shall construct, or cause or permit to be constructed, any fence composed in whole or in part of barbed wire, along the line of any street, alley or sidewalk within the city; and whoever shall violate the provisions of this section shall be deemed guilty of a misdemeanor.

(Code 1964, § 7.1160)
