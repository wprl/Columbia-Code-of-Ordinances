Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-203 Interference with legal process.


    (a) Process includes any writ, summons, subpoena, warrant other than an arrest warrant, or other process or order of a court.

    (b) A person commits the crime of interference with legal process if, knowing any person is authorized by law to serve process, for the purpose of preventing such person from effecting the service of any process, he interferes with or obstructs such person.

    (c) Interference with legal process is a class B misdemeanor.

(Code 1964, § 7.935)

     State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. § 575.160.
