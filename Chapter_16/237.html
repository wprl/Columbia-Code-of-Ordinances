Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-237 Smoking prohibited in city buses; penalty for violation of section.


    It shall be unlawful for any person to smoke on any motor bus owned or leased by the city. Violation of this section shall be deemed an infraction and punishable by a fine of not more than one hundred dollars ($100.00).

(Code 1964, § 7.1190)
