Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-285 Same--Amendments to registration statement.


    All persons using, or causing to be used, sound trucks or sound amplifying equipment for noncommercial purposes shall amend any registration statement filed pursuant to section 16-283 of this chapter within forty-eight (48) hours after any change in the information furnished in such statement.

(Code 1964, § 7.375)
