Chapter 16 OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS*


Section 16-271 Pile drivers, derricks, etc.


    The operation between the hours of 10:00 p.m. and 7:00 a.m. of any pile driver, steam shovel, pneumatic hammer, derrick, steam or electric hoist or other appliance, the use of which is attended by loud or unusual noise, is hereby declared unlawful.

(Code 1964, § 7.340)
