Chapter 7 CIVIL DEFENSE*


Section 7-17 Office and headquarters.


    The city manager shall designate space for emergency operations in any building owned or leased by the city.

(Code 1964, § 6.070; Ord. No. 10457, § 1, 2-18-85; Ord. No. 17169, § 1, 2-4-02)
