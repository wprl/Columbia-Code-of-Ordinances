Chapter 11 HEALTH AND SANITATION*


Section 11-180 Penalty.


    Any person found guilty of violating any of the provisions of sections 11-176 through 11-179 and division 2 of this article in addition to the revocation or suspension of the license as provided in section 11-202 hereof, shall, upon conviction thereof, be fined in an amount not exceeding five hundred dollars ($500.00), or be imprisoned in jail for a period of time not exceeding three (3) months, or may be both so fined and imprisoned. Each day such violation is committed or permitted to continue shall constitute a separate offense and may be punished as such.

(Code 1964, § 10.10610)
