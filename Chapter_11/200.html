Chapter 11 HEALTH AND SANITATION*


Section 11-200 Term; renewal.


    (a) Any license or renewal license issued hereunder shall be effective as of the date of its issuance and shall expire the next June thirtieth.

    (b) An applicant for a renewal license shall file with the director a written application upon forms provided by the business license administrator.

(Code 1964, § 10.10550)
