Chapter 11 HEALTH AND SANITATION*


Section 11-326 Definitions.


    The following definitions apply to this article:

     Occupant means any person eighteen (18) years of age or older who lives in a residence.

     Residence means a structure designed for human habitation. "Residence" does not include a porch or a carport.

     Residential premises means a tract or parcel of land upon which a residence is located.

(Ord. No. 13000, § 1, 6-17-91)
