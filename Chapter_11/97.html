Chapter 11 HEALTH AND SANITATION*


Section 11-97 Authority of director to require medical examination.


    Whenever it shall be deemed necessary by the director to establish the true character of any disease which is supposed to be communicable, medical examination of the person infected by such disease may be made by the director. Any person who shall interfere with or refuse to permit such examination shall be deemed to have violated the provisions of this article.

(Code 1964, § 10.190)
