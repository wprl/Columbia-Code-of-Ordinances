Chapter 11 HEALTH AND SANITATION*


Section 11-47 Officers; quorum.


    (a) The board shall select its own chair, and the director of health services shall serve as secretary.

    (b) A quorum for the transaction of business at meetings of the board shall be a majority of the duly appointed and acting members of such board.

(Code 1964, § 10.030(A), (B); Ord. No. 17658, § 1, 4-21-03)
