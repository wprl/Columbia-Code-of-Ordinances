Chapter 11 HEALTH AND SANITATION*


Section 11-295 Penalty.


    Any person violating, neglecting, or refusing to comply with any provision of this article shall be guilty of an infraction  punishable by a fine of not more than two hundred dollars ($200.00) .

(Ord. No. 11375, § 1, 2-16-87; Ord. No. 14736 § 1, 1-16-96; Ord. No. 19252. § 1, 10-9-06)

Editor's note - Ord. No. 19252, § 1, adopted October 9, 2006, which amended section 11-295 above, shall be in full force and effect from and after January 9, 2007.


(Ord. 19252, Amended, 10/09/2006, Prior Text)
