Chapter 11 HEALTH AND SANITATION*


Section 11-232 Defense.

    It is an affirmative defense to a charge of permitting, maintaining or allowing the creation or maintenance of a nuisance in violation of this division, that the defendant did not have the legal right to control the location where the alleged violation occurred.


(Code 1964, § 10.5040; Ord. No. 16982, § 2, 8-6-01)
