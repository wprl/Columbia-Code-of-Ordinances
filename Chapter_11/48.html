Chapter 11 HEALTH AND SANITATION*


Section 11-48 Duties, generally.


    The board of health shall act as an advisory board to the city manager, health officer and the city council on matters pertaining to public health and animal control and shall perform such other duties as may be prescribed by law or by ordinance.

(Code 1964, § 10.040; Ord. No. 11203, § 2, 9-15-86)
