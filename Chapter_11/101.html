Chapter 11 HEALTH AND SANITATION*


Section 11-101 Infected children not to attend school.

    No parent or guardian or any person having the legal custody, charge or control of any child shall send such child to any of the schools within the city, or within five (5) miles of the limits of the city, or permit him to enter or go to such schools after the parent, guardian or person having legal charge or control of such child has been advised that such child is affected with, or has been exposed to any such contagious or infectious disease.

(Code 1964, § 10.230)
