Chapter 11 HEALTH AND SANITATION*


Section 11-347 Defenses.


    The following are affirmative defenses to a charge of violating section 11-346:

    (1)    The defendant did not have the legal right to control the location where a violation of this article occurred.

    (2)    The unlicensed motor vehicle had been licensed within ninety (90) days prior to the violation of this article.

    (3)    The property involved is the site of a lawful motor vehicle sales or service business or a lawful junkyard.

(Ord. No. 14582 § 2, 8-21-95)
