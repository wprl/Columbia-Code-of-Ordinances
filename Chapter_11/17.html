Chapter 11 HEALTH AND SANITATION*


Section 11-17 Charges for health services.

    Patrons of the health department shall pay fees based on the current Medicaid invoice amount, with the exception of the services listed below, for personal health services.  No one will be denied health services for inability to pay.

    Dental co-pay    $10.00

    Prescription eye glass co-pay    $10.00
