Chapter 11 HEALTH AND SANITATION*


Section 11-199 Fees.


    (a) The annual fee to be paid for a junk dealer's license or renewal license issued hereunder shall be based upon gross sales, as provided by section 13-27.

    (b) All fees are due and payable upon, and computed as of the first day of business.

(Code 1964, § 10.10560)
