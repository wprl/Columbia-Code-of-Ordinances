Chapter 23 SIGNS*


Section 23-0.5 Purposes.


    The purposes of these sign regulations are:

    (1)    To allow the effective use of signs as a means of communication in the city;

    (2)    To maintain and enhance the city's ability to attract sources of economic development and growth;

    (3)    To improve pedestrian and traffic safety;

    (4)    To minimize possible adverse effects of signs on nearby property;

    (5)    To bring all signs into compliance with adopted regulations;

    (6)    To enable the fair and consistent enforcement of these sign regulations.

    (7)    To maintain and enhance the appearance of the community.

(Ord. No. 13191, § 1, 12-16-91)
