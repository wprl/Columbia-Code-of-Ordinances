Chapter 23 SIGNS*


Section 23-21 Automobile parking lot signs.


    On-premise wall or freestanding signs designating the conditions of use or identity of such parking area and not less than eighteen (18) inches by twenty-four (24) inches in dimension, nor greater than thirty-two (32) square feet in sign surface area, shall be allowed. Freestanding signs shall not be more than eight (8) feet in height, and shall be set back a minimum of ten (10) feet from the property line.

(Code 1964, § 19.890; Ord. No. 13621, § 1, 3-15-93)
