Chapter 23 SIGNS*


Section 23-6 Same--issuance.


    No permit for the erection, alteration or enlargement of any sign shall be issued by the director of community development unless:

    (1)    Application therefor has been made in accordance with the provisions of this chapter.

    (2)    The sign complies with the ordinances of the city.

    (3)    The fee for such permit has been paid.

(Code 1964, § 19.740; Ord. No. 21095, § 8, 9-19-11)
