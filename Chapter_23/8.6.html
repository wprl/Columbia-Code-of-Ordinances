Chapter 23 SIGNS*


Section 23-8.6 Temporary signs.


    Temporary signs may be allowed subject to the following conditions:

    (1)    A permit in accordance with section 23-4 shall be required.

    (2)    Such signs shall not exceed thirty-two (32) square feet in size or four (4) feet in height.

    (3)    The sign setback requirements of this chapter shall apply.

    (4)    No person shall be allowed to display a temporary sign or signs on a parcel of land for more than fourteen (14) days in a calendar year.

(Ord. No. 13191, § 1, 12-16-91)
