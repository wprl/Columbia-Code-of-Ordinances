Chapter 23 SIGNS*


Section 23-17 Civic signs.


    One on-premises freestanding sign shall be allowed for any religious or governmental entity per street block in accordance with section 23-25. One on-premises illuminated wall sign for each street frontage, shall be allowed in accordance with section 23-25.
(Code 1964, § 19.850; Ord. No. 13191, § 1, 12-16-91)
