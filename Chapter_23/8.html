Chapter 23 SIGNS*


Section 23-8 Same--revocation.


    The director of community development may revoke any permit issued by him pursuant to this chapter upon failure of the holder to comply with any of the provisions of this chapter.

(Code 1964, § 19.740; Ord. No. 21095, § 8, 9-19-11)
