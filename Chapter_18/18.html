Chapter 18 PENSIONS*


Section 18-18 Financial integrity of funds.


     The city council shall provide the necessary funds to make the police retirement plan and the firefighters’ retirement plan actuarially and financially sound for as long as the plans are maintained.

(Code 1964, § 9.1650; Ord. No. 12868, § 1, 2-4-91, Ord. No. 018160, § 1, 7-6-04)
