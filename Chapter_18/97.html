Chapter 18 PENSIONS*


Section 18-97 Workers' Compensation entitlements.

    Benefits provided under the police retirement plan shall be reduced by the amount of any Workers' Compensation entitlement in the manner set forth by these sections. Nothing herein shall be interpreted or administered to work a reduction of the total of the benefits paid and the entitlements due a covered employee below the benefit which would otherwise be payable under this division had the entitlements not been considered.

(Ord. No. 21455, § 1, 9-17-12)
