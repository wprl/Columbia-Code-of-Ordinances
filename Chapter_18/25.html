Chapter 18 PENSIONS*


Section 18-25 Diversion of funds.


    Neither the administrator, the investment board nor the respective boards of either retirement fund shall attempt to cause or permit the diversion or use of any of the assets of the retirement funds other than for the specific purposes and uses herein set forth.

(Code 1964, § 9.1720; Ord. No. 018160, § 1, 7-6-04)
