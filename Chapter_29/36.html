Chapter 29 ZONING*


Section 29-36 Permits; application, plans, etc., to be filed.


    No permit for the erection, alteration or enlargement of any building shall be issued by the director of community development unless there first be filed in the director’s office, by the applicant therefor, a plan in duplicate, drawn to scale, and in such form as may be prescribed by the director of community development , correctly showing the location and actual dimensions of the lot to be occupied, the dimensions and location of the lot of the building to be erected, altered or enlarged, together with a true statement in writing, signed by the applicant, showing the use for which such building is arranged, intended or designed, and furnishing such other information as the director of community development may require in the enforcement of the provisions of this chapter. Failure to comply with the provisions of this chapter shall be good cause for the revocation of any building permit by the director of community development . A record of such applications and plans shall be kept in the office of the director of community development .

(Code 1964, § 19.300; Ord. No. 9958, § 1, 10-3-83; Ord. No. 11702, § 1, 12-7-87; Ord. No. 21095, § 13, 9-19-11)
