Chapter 10 FRANCHISE REGULATIONS*


Section 10-3 Customer service standards.



    All video service providers providing service in the city shall adopt and comply with the minimum customer service requirements set forth in Sec. 67.2692 RSMo.  Receipt of a copy of this ordinance by the video service provider shall be deemed notice of the city invoking such customer service requirements.

(Ord. No. 18917, § 2, 3-6-06; Ord. No. 19672, § 2, 9-17-07)
