Chapter 4 ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES*


Section 4-106 Additional unlawful acts.

    (a)    It shall  be unlawful for any person to use or possess any forged or false server certificate.

    (b)    It shall be unlawful for any person to manufacture, forge, reproduce in any way or otherwise falsify a server certificate.

    (c)    It shall be unlawful for any person to give, lend, sell or otherwise provide a false server certificate to any other person.

    (d)    It shall be unlawful for any lawful holder of a server certificate to give, lend, sell or otherwise provide the server certificate to any other person.

(Ord. No. 21513, § 1, 11-19-12)


(Ord. 21513, Added, 11/19/2012)
