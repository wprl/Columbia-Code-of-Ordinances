Chapter 4 ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES*


Section 4-31 Consumption on unlicensed premises.


    It shall be unlawful for any person operating any premises where food, beverages or entertainment are sold or provided for compensation, who does not possess a license for the sale of intoxicating liquor or nonintoxicating beer, to permit the drinking or consumption of intoxicating liquor or nonintoxicating beer in the premises without having a license as provided in  Section 4-47 (10).


(Code 1964, § 4.290; Ord. No. 13733 § 1, 7-6-93; Ord. No. 17849, § 1, 9-15-03)

     State law reference(s)--Similar provisions, RSMo. 311.480(1).
