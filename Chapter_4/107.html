Chapter 4 ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES*


Section 4-107 Implementation of server certificate requirement.

    The director is authorized to implement the server certificate requirement in stages; provided, that the requirement shall be fully implemented no later than January 1, 2014.

(Ord. No. 21513, § 1, 11-19-12)
